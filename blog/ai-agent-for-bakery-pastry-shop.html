Bakers work unusual hours. You're up at 4am to get fresh bread in the oven. You're decorating cakes on Saturday when everyone else is free to browse Instagram. The very hours when potential customers are most active — evenings, weekends — are often your busiest production times. You physically cannot be on your phone responding to messages while you work.

Meanwhile, someone sees your wedding cake photo and wants to order for their event next month. If they don't get a response today, they'll look for another baker tomorrow. The AI agent closes this gap.

What an AI Agent Handles for Bakeries

  • Product availability: What you offer — breads, cakes, pastries, cookies, custom orders
  • Pricing guidance: Starting prices for different items, what affects the final cost
  • Custom order intake: Collecting event date, size, flavors, design preferences, dietary needs
  • Ordering lead time: How far in advance orders need to be placed
  • Allergen information: What items contain common allergens (nuts, gluten, dairy, eggs)
  • Pickup and delivery: Whether you deliver, delivery zones, pickup instructions
  • Operating hours: When orders can be picked up, when the shop is open

The Custom Order Flow

Custom cake orders are where bakeries make their best margins — and where the conversation is most involved. An AI agent handles the initial inquiry beautifully:

Customer: "Hi, I'm looking for a custom birthday cake for next month"

AI Agent: "How lovely! We'd love to help. To give you an accurate quote, could you tell me: the date you need it, how many servings, any flavor preferences, and if you have a design idea or theme in mind?"

The AI collects all the information you need to provide a quote. When you're done in the kitchen, you have a complete order brief waiting for you instead of having to chase down missing details through multiple message exchanges.

Handling Allergen Questions Responsibly

Allergen questions are common and critically important to answer accurately. Configure your AI agent with clear information: which items contain which allergens, whether you use shared equipment, and whether you can accommodate specific allergies. For serious allergy cases, the AI should always advise direct consultation: "For severe allergies, I recommend speaking with us directly so we can confirm the safest option for you."

Seasonal and Holiday Order Management

Bakeries experience intense demand spikes around holidays — Christmas, Easter, Valentine's Day, Mother's Day. These are exactly the periods when you're most overwhelmed and least able to respond to messages personally. The AI agent handles the surge, collecting order details and managing expectations about lead times ("We're currently fully booked for Valentine's Day. We have availability from February 20th onward — would that work for you?").
