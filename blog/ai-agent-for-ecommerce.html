Where the AI Agent Intervenes

An AI agent operates at the decision point: when a customer is considering a purchase and reaches out with a question. Handled quickly and accurately, these conversations convert hesitation into purchase. Here's how it plays out across the buying journey:

Pre-purchase Questions

"Will this fit someone who's 5'8"?" "Is this color more blue or purple in real life?" "Does it come with a warranty?" These questions, answered immediately, remove the uncertainty that causes people to abandon the purchase consideration entirely.

Shipping Concerns

"How long does delivery take?" "Do you ship to [location]?" "Can I get express delivery?" These are among the most common reasons for cart abandonment. An AI agent with your full shipping policy answers them instantly.

Return Policy Questions

Customers are more likely to buy when they know returns are easy. "What's your return policy?" is a trust question — answered clearly and quickly, it removes a major barrier to purchase.

Payment Questions

"Do you accept [payment method]?" "Can I pay in installments?" These need immediate, accurate answers or the customer leaves to find a store that's clearer about payment.
