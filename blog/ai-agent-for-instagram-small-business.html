What the AI Agent Doesn't Handle

It's important to be clear about the AI agent's limits. It handles information-based inquiries well. It doesn't handle:

For these, the AI agent can acknowledge the message and let the customer know you'll follow up personally — keeping the conversation warm while flagging it for your attention.
