Before a prospective student enrolls in any course or program, they typically go through a question funnel. "What exactly does the course cover?" "What level do I need to be?" "How long does it take?" "When are the classes?" "Is there a certificate at the end?" "What if I miss a class?" "How do I pay?"

Every unanswered question is a reason to delay or abandon enrollment. An AI agent answers all of them instantly, keeping the prospective student moving toward the enrollment decision rather than waiting for a reply that might come tomorrow.

What an AI Agent Handles for Online Schools

  • Course descriptions: What each course covers, learning outcomes, format
  • Prerequisites and levels: Who is the course for, what prior knowledge is needed
  • Duration and schedule: Length of course, class times, live vs. recorded
  • Pricing and payment: Cost, payment options, installments
  • Certificates: Whether a certificate is issued, if it's recognized
  • Technical requirements: What equipment or software students need
  • Cancellation and refund policy: What happens if a student needs to withdraw
  • Enrollment process: How to sign up, where to pay, how to access the course
