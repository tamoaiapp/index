What an AI Agent Handles for Pet Shops

  • Product availability: Which brands and products are in stock
  • Food recommendations: Which food is appropriate for specific breeds, ages, or health conditions (with appropriate caution)
  • Pricing: Product prices, grooming service prices, vaccination pricing
  • Grooming appointments: Available times, what the service includes, preparation needed
  • Vaccination and health services: What's offered, scheduling, required documents
  • Store hours and location: When you're open, where you're located
  • Delivery or click-and-collect: Whether home delivery is available
  • Breed-specific products: Items for specific animals or breeds

The Food Recommendation Challenge

Pet food questions can get complex — "My 7-year-old Labrador has joint issues, what food do you recommend?" This is where your AI agent needs to be helpful but appropriately cautious. A good approach: share the categories of food you stock that are specifically formulated for senior or joint-health dogs, and suggest the customer speak with your staff or their vet for a specific recommendation for their pet's situation.

This combination — helpful context plus responsible escalation — serves customers well and protects you from giving advice that might not apply to their specific animal.

Grooming Bookings via WhatsApp
