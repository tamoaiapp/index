WhatsApp has over 2 billion active users globally, and in many markets — particularly Brazil, India, Southeast Asia, Africa, and Latin America — it's the primary way customers communicate with businesses. For small businesses in these markets, WhatsApp is customer service.

Customers use WhatsApp to ask product questions, place orders, confirm deliveries, request quotes, and handle complaints. They expect the same responsiveness they'd get from a friend. This is both an opportunity (intimate, direct selling) and a challenge (constant availability required).

Personal WhatsApp vs. WhatsApp Business

TamoWork works with both regular WhatsApp and WhatsApp Business. WhatsApp Business offers some additional features (business profile, quick replies, labels) that can complement your AI agent. However, you don't need WhatsApp Business to use TamoWork — regular WhatsApp works perfectly well.

The WhatsApp Business API (used by large enterprises with complex automation) is not required. TamoWork uses a direct connection approach that works for small business operators without API access.

Setting Response Expectations

One consideration: customers will receive responses very quickly — often within seconds — at all hours. This is generally a positive experience. However, if the AI is responding at 3am and a customer follows up expecting immediate further responses (e.g., to confirm a payment), they might be confused about the availability of a human.

Best practice: the AI agent's signature or responses can include a note about human availability: "Our AI employee replies 24/7. For anything that needs personal attention, our team is available Mon–Sat 9am–6pm."

Handling the WhatsApp Order Flow

For businesses that take orders via WhatsApp, the AI agent is particularly valuable. It manages the entire order intake conversation:

  1. Customer asks about a product
  2. AI provides product details and price
  3. Customer expresses interest in ordering
  4. AI asks for size, color, quantity, delivery address
  5. AI confirms the order details and payment process
  6. Order brief lands in your inbox ready to fulfill

You wake up to a queue of completed order intakes rather than a mess of incomplete conversations to untangle.

Privacy Considerations for WhatsApp AI

WhatsApp messages are end-to-end encrypted between sender and recipient. When TamoWork processes them, it does so locally on your computer — the messages are decrypted on your device (as they would be normally), processed by the local AI, and responses are sent. No third-party cloud service processes the content of your WhatsApp messages. Your conversations remain private.
