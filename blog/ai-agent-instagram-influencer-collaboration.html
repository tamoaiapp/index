Preparing Your AI Agent Before the Campaign Goes Live

The time to set up and test your AI agent is before the influencer posts — not after messages start coming in. Here is what to prepare:

  • Campaign-specific context: Tell your agent about the campaign. Which product is being featured? Is there a special offer for the influencer's followers? What is the influencer's name so the agent can acknowledge the connection naturally?
  • Product knowledge: Ensure your agent has complete, accurate information about the featured product — all variants, pricing, shipping times, and answers to likely questions.
  • Special offer handling: If you are offering a discount code or special bundle for the campaign, make sure the agent knows how to share it and confirm it with customers.
  • Stock awareness: If your product is limited quantity, program the agent with a plan for when stock runs out — a waitlist, alternative products, or a clear "currently sold out" message.
