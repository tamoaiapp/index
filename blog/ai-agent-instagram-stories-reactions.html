Types of Stories That Generate the Best Reactions

Not all Stories produce the same volume or quality of reactions. If you want to maximize the value of your AI agent, it helps to post Stories that naturally invite engagement. The highest-performing formats for small businesses include:

Product spotlights with urgency

A short video or image showing a product with text like "Last 5 in stock" or "New arrival — reacting with a fire means you want details" trains your audience to react when they are interested. The AI agent then catches every one of those signals.

Poll-style content

Even when you use Instagram's built-in poll sticker, many viewers prefer to react with an emoji instead of tapping a poll option. Your AI agent can respond to both, making sure no signal goes unnoticed.

Behind-the-scenes content

Stories showing your process, your workspace, or how a product is made tend to generate strong emotional reactions. Someone who reacts to a behind-the-scenes Story is expressing trust and connection — an AI agent can turn that into a deeper conversation about your brand.

Flash sale announcements

Posting a Story about a limited-time offer and inviting reactions as a way to "claim" the deal is a simple and effective tactic. The AI agent then handles all the follow-up automatically, sending order details or payment links to everyone who reacted.
