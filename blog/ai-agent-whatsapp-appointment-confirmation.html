When a customer messages asking to book an appointment, the AI agent takes them through a structured but friendly booking flow:

  1. Service selection — "What service are you looking to book? We offer [list your services]"
  2. Date and time preference — "What days and times work best for you? We're available [your hours/days]"
  3. Confirmation of availability — "Great — I have [date] at [time] available. Does that work for you?"
  4. Details collection — Name, any relevant notes (allergies for a beauty service, specific issues for a repair service)
  5. Booking confirmation — A complete summary: "Your appointment is confirmed for [date] at [time] for [service]. Our address is [address]. See you then!"

This entire flow happens automatically. You see the completed booking in your TamoWork log and simply add it to your calendar. The customer experience is seamless and professional.

Appointment Reminder Messages

No-shows are one of the most costly problems for service businesses. A client who does not show up costs you the slot, the opportunity to book someone else, and potentially prep time that went to waste. The most effective way to reduce no-shows is timely reminders — and most businesses underdo this because it requires manual effort.

Your AI agent can send automated reminder messages at defined intervals before each appointment:

  • 24-hour reminder — "Just a reminder that your appointment is tomorrow at [time]. Reply YES to confirm or let us know if you need to reschedule."
  • 2-hour reminder (optional for same-day appointments) — "See you in a couple of hours! We're at [address]. Looking forward to it."

Requiring clients to actively confirm 24 hours ahead — with the AI agent processing their "YES" reply automatically — catches many potential no-shows before they happen. If a client does not confirm, the AI agent can follow up and offer a rescheduling option, keeping the slot either filled or made available for another booking.

Handling Cancellations and Rescheduling

Cancellations are inevitable. How you handle them determines whether you retain the client relationship. An AI agent handles cancellation messages with grace: acknowledging the cancellation, expressing genuine understanding, and immediately offering rescheduling options.

"No problem at all — life happens! We'd love to rebook you when you're ready. I have availability on [day] at [time] or [day] at [time] — do either of those work?" This message converts a cancellation into a rescheduling opportunity rather than a lost customer.

If cancellations happen less than a defined threshold before the appointment (say, under 24 hours), your AI agent can be configured to mention your cancellation policy without being punitive about it. This educates clients about your policy while keeping the relationship intact.

Post-Appointment Follow-Up

The appointment does not end when the client leaves. A timely post-appointment message from your AI agent reinforces the positive experience and opens the door to rebooking: "Hi [name]! We hope your [service] went well — it was lovely having you. Would you like to book your next appointment now? We can hold your preferred time."

This follow-up, sent automatically a day or two after the appointment, generates a consistent rebooking rate that builds your recurring revenue without requiring you to manually chase every client.
