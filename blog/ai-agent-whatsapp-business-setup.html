Setting Up: What You Need Before You Start

Before connecting your AI agent to WhatsApp Business, make sure you have the basics in place:

  • WhatsApp Business installed — available for free on Android and iOS
  • Business profile completed — name, category, description, hours, address, website
  • Your product catalog or service list ready — this is the information your AI agent will draw from when answering customer questions
  • TamoWork installed on your computer — download at tamowork.com

The setup process in TamoWork is designed to be straightforward. You connect your WhatsApp Business account, provide context about your business and what you sell, define your tone of voice, and set any specific rules for how the AI agent should handle particular types of conversations.

The Context Configuration: The Most Important Step

The quality of your AI agent's responses depends entirely on the quality of the context you provide during setup. Think of it as writing a comprehensive briefing document for a new employee. The more specific and complete your briefing, the better the employee performs.

Key information to include in your AI agent context:

  • What you sell — product names, descriptions, prices, variants, and availability
  • How customers buy — your ordering process, payment methods, and checkout flow
  • Delivery information — shipping options, timelines, and costs
  • Return and exchange policy
  • Common questions and your standard answers
  • Your tone — how formal or casual you want the AI agent to sound
  • Business hours — so the AI agent can let customers know when to expect personal follow-up for complex issues

Testing Before Going Live

Before letting your AI agent handle real customer conversations unsupervised, spend some time testing it. Send test messages that mimic real customer questions and review the responses. This helps you identify gaps in your context configuration and refine the AI agent's behavior before customers experience it.

Common things to test:

  • Basic product questions — does the AI agent have accurate information?
  • Price inquiries — does it respond correctly?
  • Complaint scenarios — does it handle them with the right tone?
  • Out-of-scope questions — does it appropriately acknowledge limits and offer to escalate?
  • Ordering process questions — does it guide customers correctly?

Monitoring and Refining Over Time

Once your AI agent is live, monitor its conversations regularly in the early days. TamoWork logs every conversation, so you can review how the AI agent is performing and identify any situations it is handling poorly. Use these observations to refine your context configuration and improve performance over time.

Most businesses find that the AI agent needs only minor adjustments in the first week — adding information about a product the AI agent did not know about, clarifying a policy that was ambiguous, or updating pricing that has changed. After the initial refinement period, the AI agent typically runs smoothly with minimal ongoing maintenance.
