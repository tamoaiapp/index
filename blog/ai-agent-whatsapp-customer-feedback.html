When to Ask for Feedback

Timing is critical. Ask too soon and the customer hasn't had a chance to form an opinion. Ask too late and the experience has faded from memory. The optimal windows are:

  • 24–48 hours after delivery for physical products — enough time to receive, open, and try the item
  • Same day, a few hours after for services — while the experience is still fresh
  • End of an appointment or service session for in-person businesses — while the customer is still in a good mood from the interaction

An AI agent sends the feedback request automatically at the configured time, without you needing to remember or manually trigger anything.

How to Ask for Feedback Without Being Annoying

The format of the request matters as much as the timing. Long surveys kill response rates. A simple, conversational question on WhatsApp works far better.

Effective feedback request formats

  • Simple rating: "Hi! How would you rate your experience with us today? 😊 Reply with a number from 1 to 5."
  • Open question: "Hey, how did everything go with your order? Any feedback is welcome — we love hearing from you."
  • Specific question: "Was the delivery time what you expected? We're always looking to improve."

Keep it to one question. If the customer gives a detailed response, the AI agent can ask a natural follow-up — but the initial ask should be low-effort.

What the AI Agent Does with the Response

This is where automation becomes genuinely powerful. When a customer leaves positive feedback, the AI agent can:

  • Thank them warmly and invite them to share their experience on Google or Instagram
  • Offer a small loyalty reward or discount for their next purchase
  • Ask if they'd be comfortable with their feedback being shared as a testimonial

When a customer leaves negative feedback, the AI agent can:

  • Apologize sincerely and ask what went wrong
  • Offer a solution — replacement, refund, or a free follow-up service
  • Flag the conversation for you to review personally

Handling negative feedback quickly and professionally is one of the highest-impact things a business can do for retention. A dissatisfied customer who gets a fast, empathetic response often becomes one of the most loyal customers you have.

Turning Feedback into Reviews

Happy customers rarely leave reviews on their own — they need a gentle nudge at the right moment. After a customer gives positive feedback via WhatsApp, an AI agent can send a direct link to your Google Business profile or Instagram page and ask them to share their experience publicly.

This converts private satisfaction into visible social proof, which directly impacts how many new customers find and trust your business. The entire process — from feedback request to review invitation — happens automatically.
