There is a well-documented phenomenon in sales called purchase momentum. Once a customer has made the decision to buy, they want to complete the transaction quickly. Every step that introduces friction, delay, or confusion erodes this momentum. The more time passes between "I'll take it" and "payment received," the higher the chance of abandonment.

An AI agent that sends payment information instantly — without waiting for you to see the message, grab the link, and type a reply — maintains purchase momentum at its peak. The customer receives payment details while they are still mentally committed to buying, and the transaction is completed before hesitation has a chance to set in.

Types of Payment Information Your AI Agent Can Share

The specific payment information your AI agent needs to share depends on your payment methods. Common formats include:

Payment links

If you use a payment platform (Stripe, PayPal, MercadoPago, or similar), you can configure your AI agent with standard payment links for your most common products or order values. When a customer confirms their order, the AI agent sends the appropriate link: "Great choice! Here is your payment link: [link]. This is valid for 24 hours. Let me know once you've completed it and I'll confirm your order right away."

Bank transfer details

For businesses that collect payment via bank transfer, the AI agent sends the account details along with instructions for the reference (usually the customer's name or order number): "To complete your order, please transfer $[amount] to: [bank name], [account number], [routing info]. Use your name as the reference so we can match your payment. Send me the receipt once done!"

PIX or digital wallet

For markets where instant digital payment systems are common, the AI agent shares the appropriate key or QR code reference with clear instructions on the amount and reference to use.

Confirming Received Payments

After sending payment information, your AI agent can be configured to watch for payment confirmation messages. When a customer replies with "Payment sent!" or sends a receipt screenshot, the AI agent acknowledges receipt: "Got it, thank you! Your payment has been received and your order is confirmed. [Order summary]. We'll get this out to you by [date]. Thanks for shopping with us!"

This immediate acknowledgment eliminates the anxiety of "did they receive my payment?" that causes customers to follow up repeatedly. The customer feels secure, and you do not have to manually respond to every payment confirmation message.

Handling Payment Delays and Abandoned Payments

Not every customer who receives a payment link completes the transaction immediately. Some get distracted, have second thoughts, or experience technical issues. Your AI agent can send a gentle follow-up after a defined time period if no payment confirmation is received:

"Hi! Just checking in — did you have any trouble with the payment link I sent? Sometimes these things have a short expiry. If the link has expired, I can send a fresh one — just let me know!"

This message is helpful, not pushy. It addresses the most common reason for non-completion (technical issue or expired link) and makes it easy for the customer to proceed. A significant percentage of follow-ups like this result in completed payments that would otherwise have been abandoned.

Building Trust Around Payment Security

Some customers, particularly first-time buyers, feel nervous about sending payment to a business they have just discovered. Your AI agent can proactively address this concern before it becomes a barrier: "This is a standard secure payment process — we've completed over [X] transactions this way. If you're ever unsure, you're welcome to make a small test payment first. Your trust matters to us."

For businesses accepting bank transfers specifically, including your business name and registered information in the payment instructions — and having your AI agent reference your social media profile or website as verification — helps customers feel confident that they are paying the right person.
