What "Inactive" Actually Means

Before setting up any re-engagement campaign, define what inactive means for your business:

  • 30 days: Appropriate for businesses with frequent purchases (food, beauty, consumables)
  • 60–90 days: Better for services with regular cycles (cleaning, maintenance, coaching)
  • 6 months+: For businesses with longer purchase cycles (furniture, electronics, courses)

The right threshold depends on how often a typical customer buys from you. Setting it too short makes the outreach feel spammy; too long and the customer may have already moved on permanently.

What an AI Agent Sends to Win Customers Back

The key to re-engagement is making the message feel personal and valuable — not like mass marketing. An AI agent achieves this by referencing context from previous interactions and offering something concrete.

Three proven re-engagement message types

  • The "we miss you" check-in: A friendly message acknowledging the gap and asking if there's anything the customer needs. Low pressure, high warmth.
  • The exclusive returning-customer offer: A discount or bonus that's specifically for customers who've purchased before. Creates urgency without being aggressive.
  • The new product/service announcement: Telling the customer about something new that's relevant to what they bought previously. Feels informative rather than sales-y.

An AI agent can handle incoming replies to these messages automatically — answering questions, confirming interest, and guiding the customer back toward a new purchase.

Best Practices for WhatsApp Re-engagement

  • Keep messages short: Long re-engagement messages feel like spam. Two or three sentences is enough to spark interest.
  • Use the customer's name: Personalization increases open and response rates significantly.
  • Give a reason to reply: Ask a question or make an offer that requires a response. Don't just broadcast a message and hope for the best.
  • Don't follow up more than twice: If a customer doesn't respond to two messages, they've made their decision. Respecting that keeps your reputation intact.
  • Time it thoughtfully: Sending messages mid-morning on weekdays tends to get higher engagement than evenings or weekends.
