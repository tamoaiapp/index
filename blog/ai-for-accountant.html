Accounting is a high-trust, high-retention business. Clients who find an accountant they trust stick with them for years, sometimes decades. The acquisition of a new client is therefore an investment that pays for itself many times over. But many accountants inadvertently create barriers to that acquisition by being difficult to reach — especially during the periods when they're most in demand.

Small business owners, freelancers, and individuals searching for accounting help often message multiple CPAs or bookkeepers at once. The first one to respond with clear information about their services, fees, and availability gets the consultation. The others don't.

What an AI Employee Does for Accounting Practices

TamoWork gives your accounting practice an AI employee that handles WhatsApp and Instagram inquiries 24/7. It's completely free, runs on your computer, and doesn't require any technical setup beyond the initial configuration.

Service type explanation

Prospects often don't know exactly what services they need. Do they need a bookkeeper or a CPA? Tax preparation or tax planning? Payroll services? Business formation? Your AI employee can explain the difference between your service offerings and help prospects identify what's appropriate for their situation, making initial consultations more productive.

Deadline and timing questions

Tax deadlines generate a flood of urgent inquiries, and "can you still help me?" messages are common during crunch periods. Your AI employee can communicate your current capacity, whether you're still accepting new clients for the current filing season, and what the realistic timelines are for completing returns under different scenarios.

Fee structure communication

Accounting fees are often opaque to prospective clients. Your AI employee can explain how you charge — hourly rates, fixed fees for specific services, monthly retainers for ongoing bookkeeping — so prospects come to consultations with a realistic sense of cost. This pre-qualifies clients by budget and reduces awkward conversations about fees late in the process.

Consultation scheduling

The goal of most initial inquiries is to book a discovery call or meeting. Your AI employee can handle this scheduling process — collecting basic information about the prospect's needs and business type, and confirming consultation slots — so your calendar fills with qualified appointments even when you're heads-down on client work.
