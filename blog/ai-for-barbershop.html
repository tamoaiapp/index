Walk-In vs. Appointment — How AI Handles the Nuance

One common concern for barbershop owners is the walk-in versus appointment dynamic. Many shops run primarily on walk-ins and do not want clients to think they need to book in advance. Others take appointments for certain services but keep the majority of chairs open for walk-ins.

TamoWork handles this nuance well because you write the rules. You can tell it to explain your walk-in policy clearly, to suggest that clients arrive at off-peak times for shorter waits, or to direct specific service inquiries (like a full color) to a booking link. The AI employee follows your instructions precisely.
