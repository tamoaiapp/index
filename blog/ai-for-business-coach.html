The Lead Conversion Problem for Business Coaches

Business coaching leads are active, motivated, and often evaluating multiple coaches simultaneously. When someone messages you on Instagram after watching your content about scaling a service business, they're in decision mode. They want a signal that you're organized, responsive, and professional. A delayed reply sends exactly the opposite signal.

The pre-qualification questions your leads ask are remarkably consistent:

  • What type of businesses do you specialize in coaching?
  • What does a coaching engagement look like?
  • How long is your program?
  • Do you do group coaching, 1-on-1, or both?
  • What results do your clients typically achieve?
  • What is your investment level?
  • Do you offer a discovery call?
  • How many clients do you work with at once?

These are not questions that require your personal wisdom to answer. They're logistical questions that an AI employee can handle perfectly — giving leads the information they need to decide whether to book a discovery call with you.
