Setting Up Your AI Employee for Career Counseling

Define your specialty and client profile

Write a clear description of who you help best: career changers, recent graduates, executives in transition, professionals returning after a career break. The more specific your positioning, the more your AI employee can pre-qualify leads and attract the right clients.

List your services clearly

Document every service you offer: career direction counseling, resume and LinkedIn optimization, interview preparation, salary negotiation coaching, and any industry-specific packages. Include pricing and session formats for each.

Communicate your credentials

Prospective clients care about your qualifications — certifications, years of experience, industry background, notable client outcomes. Configure your AI employee to share these naturally in conversation to build trust before the first session.

Offer a low-risk first step

A free 15 or 20-minute discovery call dramatically increases the conversion rate from inquiry to paid client. Make this the standard call to action in every automated response.
