What Your Catering AI Employee Handles

Initial Lead Capture

When a potential client messages "I'm looking for a caterer for my company's annual dinner for 80 people," your AI employee immediately engages: acknowledges the inquiry, expresses enthusiasm about the event, and begins collecting the details needed for a quote. The lead is captured and qualified while you're busy running another event.

Menu Package Descriptions

Most catering companies offer tiered packages — a buffet option, a plated service option, a cocktail reception option. Your AI employee presents these clearly, explains what's included, and answers questions about customization. Clients get the information they need to make a preliminary decision before ever speaking with you.

Availability and Logistics Questions

"Do you cater outdoor events?" "Can you accommodate a Kosher menu?" "Do you provide staff?" "What's your minimum guest count?" These questions have consistent answers that your AI employee handles efficiently, helping clients understand whether you're the right fit before investing time in a detailed quote process.

Pricing Framework

Exact catering quotes require full event details, but you can provide ballpark ranges — "Our plated dinner packages typically start at $45 per person for groups of 50+" — that help clients assess whether your services fit their budget without requiring a full consultation upfront.

Setting Up Your Catering AI Employee

  1. Define your service offerings clearly. What types of events do you cater? What cuisine styles? What service formats (buffet, plated, cocktail)?
  2. Build your package descriptions. Tier your offerings with clear descriptions of what's included at each level.
  3. Write your qualifying questions. What do you need to know before you can provide a quote? Build these into the AI's intake flow.
  4. Set your pricing framework. Even rough ranges help clients self-qualify before taking up your time.
  5. Configure TamoWork and test. Run through an inquiry as a potential client, verifying that leads are captured completely and professionally.
