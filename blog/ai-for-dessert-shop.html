Dessert shops have perhaps the most natural relationship with Instagram of any food business. An AI employee ensures that every sales opportunity your Instagram creates gets captured immediately — even when you're wrist-deep in fondant working on someone else's order.

The Custom Order Problem for Dessert Shops

Custom dessert orders are information-intensive. Before you can quote or even confirm feasibility, you need to know: What's the occasion? When is it needed? How many servings? What flavors does the customer like? Do they have any allergies? What aesthetic are they going for? Do they want delivery or pickup?

Collecting all this information manually, message by message, is slow and often results in incomplete information anyway — because customers answer questions and then disappear, leaving you waiting for the final detail you need to provide a quote.

An AI employee structures this intake process systematically: it asks the right questions in the right order, collects complete information, and delivers a fully qualified lead to you. Instead of 8 fragmented messages over 24 hours, you receive one complete order summary ready for you to quote.

What Your Dessert Shop AI Employee Handles

Custom Order Intake

The complete intake flow for custom dessert orders — occasion, date, size, flavors, design inspiration, dietary requirements, delivery information. The AI employee guides customers through this process conversationally, so it feels natural rather than like filling out a form. By the time you see the conversation, everything you need is already there.

Pricing Guidance

Custom dessert pricing is complex because it varies significantly by size, complexity, and ingredients. Your AI employee provides pricing frameworks: "Custom cakes typically start at $X for a 6-inch cake serving 8–10 people, with pricing increasing based on design complexity and size. For a personalized quote, I'll need a few more details." This sets appropriate expectations while collecting the information needed for accurate pricing.

Flavor and Design Options

Customers often don't know exactly what they want — they know they want "something chocolate" or "something elegant for a wedding." Your AI employee can present your signature flavor combinations and design styles, providing inspiration that helps customers crystallize their vision and makes the ordering process easier for everyone.

Ready-Made and In-Store Offerings

Not every customer wants a custom order — some want to know what you have available for same-day pickup. Your AI employee handles these inquiries too, sharing your current in-store selection and any daily specials available for immediate purchase.

Setting Up Your Dessert Shop AI Employee

  1. Build your custom order intake questionnaire. List every question you need answered to provide a quote. This becomes the AI's intake flow.
  2. Create your pricing framework. Base prices by size, complexity tiers, flavor premiums. Enough to set expectations without committing to a final price prematurely.
  3. Document your signature flavors and design styles. Help customers choose by giving them clear options with enticing descriptions.
  4. Set your lead times and policies. Minimum notice required for custom orders, rush order policy, deposit requirements.
  5. Test the custom order flow thoroughly. Run through multiple order types — a simple birthday cake, a complex wedding cake, a last-minute request — and verify the intake is complete and accurate each time.
