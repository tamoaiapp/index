Why Etsy Sellers Need Instagram and WhatsApp

Building an Instagram following and a WhatsApp contact list changes this dynamic. The challenge is that maintaining these channels requires responsive communication — something that's hard to sustain manually when you're also making products, managing Etsy orders, and running a business.
