What Fishing Store Customers Ask

Fishing customers tend to be passionate and knowledgeable about their sport, which means their questions are specific and often time-sensitive:

  • Do you have live bait available this morning?
  • Do you carry [specific brand/model] fishing rods?
  • What are the current fishing conditions at [local spot]?
  • Do you sell fishing licenses?
  • Do you have [specific lure color/size] in stock?
  • What line do you recommend for [species/technique]?
  • Do you offer rod repair or reel servicing?
  • Are you open early on Saturdays?

Most of these questions have immediate, factual answers. An AI employee configured with your store's inventory, services, and hours can respond to all of them instantly.
