What an AI Employee Does for Freelancers

TamoWork gives you an AI employee that handles WhatsApp and Instagram messages 24/7 — at no cost, running on your own computer, with no subscription required. Here's what it specifically handles for freelancers:

Quote and project inquiry handling

When a potential client messages "I need a [project type], what do you charge?", your AI employee doesn't just say you'll get back to them. It asks the right discovery questions: project scope, timeline, deliverables, budget range, and any specific requirements. By the time you follow up, you have enough information to provide a realistic quote on the first response — which dramatically increases conversion rates.

Availability communication

Nothing frustrates a potential client more than vague availability. "I'll check and get back to you" is a response that invites them to find someone more organized. Your AI employee can communicate your current availability status clearly: "I'm currently fully booked through [date] and taking new projects for [next period]." Clear, professional, immediate.

Portfolio and past work questions

Prospects frequently ask "have you done X before?" or "can I see examples of [type of work]?" Your AI employee can describe your relevant experience and direct them to your portfolio on Instagram, your website, or wherever you showcase your work. This keeps the discovery process moving without you having to personally curate portfolio selections for every inquiry.

Process and working style questions

Smart clients want to understand how you work before hiring you. What does your project process look like? How do revisions work? What do you need from the client upfront? Your AI employee can explain your working process in detail, setting expectations before the first conversation and screening out clients who aren't a good fit.
