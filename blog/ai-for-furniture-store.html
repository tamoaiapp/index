During this extended decision cycle, every interaction with your store either strengthens or weakens their purchase intent.

An AI employee that responds instantly to every inquiry — whether it's the first question or the fifteenth — keeps customers engaged with your store throughout that entire research phase, dramatically increasing the likelihood that they choose you when they're finally ready to buy.

What Your AI Employee Handles

Dimensions and Measurements

Furniture customers are obsessive about dimensions. They're measuring their spaces and comparing against product measurements constantly. Your AI employee can provide precise dimensions for every piece you sell — width, depth, height, seat height, weight capacity — giving customers the information they need to make confident decisions without waiting for a callback.

Materials and Durability

Material questions are common and important: Is this solid wood or MDF? What type of upholstery is used? How does it hold up with pets or children? Is this leather genuine or bonded? Your AI employee can answer material and durability questions thoroughly, helping customers understand exactly what they're investing in.

Delivery and Assembly

Furniture logistics are a common concern. Your AI employee can explain delivery timelines, delivery area coverage, assembly service options, cost of delivery, and what happens if an item is damaged during transport. Clear answers to these practical concerns remove major barriers to purchase.

Customization Options

Many furniture buyers want something specific — a color that matches their walls, a fabric that works with their pets, a size that fits their unusual space. Your AI employee can explain what customization options are available, how to place a custom order, lead times for custom pieces, and any price adjustments for non-standard options.
