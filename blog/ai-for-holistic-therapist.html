The Unique Inquiry Landscape for Holistic Practices

Holistic therapy clients often come with more questions than typical healthcare patients. They're exploring unfamiliar modalities, curious about what to expect, wondering if a particular practice is right for them. This curiosity is an opportunity — the more questions you can answer quickly, the faster you build trust and move toward a booking.

Common inquiries for holistic therapy practices include:

  • "What is a Reiki session like?" — The AI describes a typical session in your style.
  • "Can crystal healing help with anxiety?" — It explains your approach and what clients typically experience.
  • "How many sessions do I need?" — The AI gives a helpful general answer and recommends an initial consultation.
  • "Do you offer distance healing sessions?" — It explains your virtual offerings.
  • "What should I wear / eat before a session?" — Practical preparation guidance.
  • "How do I book?" — Immediate, clear booking instructions.
