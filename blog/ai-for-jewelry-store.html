Setting Up Your Jewelry Store AI Employee

Step 1: Create Complete Material Documentation

For every piece in your collection, document: the complete material description (metal type and purity, gemstone type and origin, coating type if applicable), allergy information (nickel-free, hypoallergenic status), durability notes (shower-safe? chlorine-resistant?), and care instructions. This is the most critical knowledge for jewelry customer service.

Step 2: Document Your Personalization Options

Write out all personalization options in detail: which pieces can be engraved or personalized, what fonts are available, character limits, any additional cost, lead time for personalized orders, and how customers submit their personalization request. Clear, complete personalization information drives one of jewelry's highest-value purchase categories.

Step 3: Build a Gift Guide by Occasion and Budget

Create a reference guide organized by gift occasion (birthday, anniversary, graduation, Mother's Day, Valentine's Day) and budget range ($30-50, $50-100, $100+). This gives your AI employee the ability to make specific gift recommendations immediately when a customer describes what they're looking for — turning a vague "looking for a gift" into a concrete suggestion that drives purchase.
