What an AI Employee Handles for Landscapers

TamoWork gives your landscaping business an AI employee that handles incoming messages on WhatsApp and Instagram around the clock. Here's what it manages specifically for landscaping businesses:

Service menu and pricing questions

Customers frequently ask "do you do X?" or "how much for Y?" Your AI employee knows your service list — lawn mowing, hedge trimming, garden design, irrigation, mulching, seasonal cleanups — and can answer clearly, including approximate pricing ranges where relevant.

Seasonal service explanations

Spring cleanup, fall leaf removal, winterization — many customers don't know what they need or when. Your AI employee can explain seasonal services in plain language, helping customers understand the value of scheduling in advance.

Availability and scheduling

During peak periods, your calendar fills fast. The AI can communicate your current lead time accurately — "we're booking 2–3 weeks out right now" — so customers have realistic expectations rather than assuming you can come tomorrow.

Property detail collection

For quote requests, the AI asks about property size, existing landscape features, access, and specific concerns. When you follow up, you have real data to work with instead of a vague "can you come give me a quote?"

Instagram engagement

Your before-and-after photos and garden transformation posts attract significant engagement. When people comment asking about your services or send a DM, the AI responds immediately and moves them toward booking a consultation.
