AI for Life Coaches: Automate Client Inquiries 24/7

🗓 March 22, 2026⏱ 7 min read

You became a life coach to transform people's lives — not to spend hours each day answering the same questions on Instagram and WhatsApp. Yet here you are, glued to your phone, replying to "How much do your sessions cost?" and "Do you offer online coaching?" while your actual clients wait for your full attention.

This is the hidden cost of running a solo coaching practice. Every minute spent on repetitive inquiries is a minute stolen from deep work, client sessions, and building the programs that actually grow your business. And the problem compounds: when you're slow to reply, potential clients move on to the next coach who responded first.

The Inquiry Problem Every Life Coach Faces

Research consistently shows that leads contacted within five minutes are dramatically more likely to convert than those contacted after an hour. As a life coach, your leads often come through Instagram — someone sees your post about mindset shifts, gets inspired, and sends you a DM. If you're in a session, asleep, or simply overwhelmed, that message sits unanswered. By the time you reply, their emotional momentum has faded.

The inquiries themselves are predictable. Across hundreds of coaching businesses, the same questions appear over and over:

  • What does a session with you look like?
  • How many sessions do I need to see results?
  • Do you work with clients online or only in person?
  • What's your pricing?
  • Do you offer a discovery call?
  • What areas of life do you specialize in?

These questions deserve good answers — but they don't require you personally to answer them at 11pm. An AI employee can handle them instantly, professionally, and consistently.
