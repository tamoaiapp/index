How to Configure TamoWork for Your Makeup School

Document every course in detail

For each program you offer, write out the full curriculum overview, duration, schedule, what's included (kit, products, manual), pricing and payment options, and any prerequisites. Include your certification or diploma details — aspiring professionals care about credentials.

Address the career question proactively

Many prospects ask about career outcomes. Configure your AI employee to mention your job placement support, industry connections, alumni success stories, and the earning potential of professional makeup artists. This transforms a course inquiry into a career conversation — much more compelling.

Create a clear enrollment funnel

Every conversation should end with an invitation to the next step: a school visit, a demo class, or a registration form. Configure your AI employee to always close with a specific, easy action — not just "let us know if you have more questions."

Handle Instagram comment replies

When someone comments on your post asking "How do I sign up?" or "Is this something you teach?", your AI employee can reply immediately in the comments, directing them to DM for full details. This keeps your comment section active and professional.
