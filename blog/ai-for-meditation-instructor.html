Practical Setup for Your Practice

Write your schedule and pricing clearly

Start by writing down every piece of information a new student might need: class days and times, session length, meditation styles you teach, pricing for single sessions and packages, whether classes are online or in person, and how to register. This becomes the foundation of your AI employee's knowledge.

Set the right tone

Your AI employee should sound like you — warm, calm, welcoming. Take a few minutes during setup to describe how you communicate. Phrases like "gentle, encouraging, and non-intimidating" give the AI clear direction.

Handle your full classes smartly

If a class is fully booked, configure the AI employee to let people know and offer to add them to a waitlist or point them to the next available session. This prevents disappointment and keeps potential students engaged.

Keep you in the loop

Configure escalation triggers for unusual situations — a student reporting health concerns that might affect participation, requests for private sessions, or corporate wellness inquiries. These reach you directly while routine questions are handled automatically.
