Questions Your AI Employee Handles Automatically

Instruments and Programs Available

Parents frequently ask "Do you teach violin?" or "Do you have drum lessons for kids?" Your AI employee can provide an instant, complete list of every instrument and program your school offers, including any specialty programs like music theory, ear training, or ensemble groups.

Skill Levels and Age Groups

Prospective students range from complete beginners to those seeking advanced training. Your AI employee can explain how your school assesses skill levels, what beginner programs look like, and whether you have specific programs for children, teenagers, or adults.

Pricing and Monthly Fees

Tuition questions are among the most common. Your AI employee can clearly communicate monthly fees, what's included (number of lessons per month, lesson duration, any additional materials), and whether you offer sibling discounts, referral programs, or trial class promotions.

Schedule and Lesson Format

Parents need to know when lessons are available before they can commit. Your AI employee can share available time slots, explain whether lessons are individual or group, describe lesson duration, and clarify how makeup lessons work if a student needs to miss a session.

Trial classes are one of the most effective enrollment tools for music schools.
