Building a nutrition practice requires a continuous flow of new inquiries. Unlike a gym or salon where clients might walk in off the street, nutritionists rely heavily on their online presence and word-of-mouth to attract clients. Instagram is a primary discovery channel — but discovery only converts to clients when the inquiry experience is fast and professional.

The questions potential nutrition clients ask are consistent: "Do you specialize in weight loss, or do you work with eating disorders too?" "Is it possible to do consultations online?" "What does a first appointment look like?" "How many sessions do I need to see results?" "Do you work with specific conditions like PCOS or IBS?"

These questions, answered quickly and thoroughly, move people from curious to committed. An AI employee handles this work automatically, so your pipeline stays active even when you are in session or offline.
