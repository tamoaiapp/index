How an AI Employee Sells Your Courses

An AI employee doesn't just answer questions — it guides prospects through the entire pre-purchase journey. When someone comments on your Instagram post saying "I'm interested," your AI employee can immediately send them a DM with course details, pricing, and a purchase link. When they ask follow-up questions, it answers them accurately and promptly.

After purchase, the same AI employee handles access questions: how to log in, where to find the materials, how to join the community group, and what to do if they encounter technical issues. This level of post-purchase support dramatically reduces refund requests because students feel supported rather than abandoned.

Questions Your AI Employee Can Handle

Course Content and Curriculum

Prospective students want to know exactly what they're getting. Your AI employee can describe each module, explain how many hours of video are included, list any bonuses or downloadable materials, and clarify what skills students will have by the end of the course.

Pricing and Payment Plans

Price is often the biggest objection. Your AI employee can share the full price, any available installment options, limited-time promotions, and what's included at each price tier if you have multiple packages.

Access and Platform

Students frequently ask how they'll access the course after buying. Your AI employee can explain your hosting platform, how login credentials are sent, whether access is lifetime or time-limited, and how to use the platform on mobile devices.

Refund Policy and Guarantees

Sharing your refund policy proactively builds trust. Your AI employee can explain any money-back guarantees, what the terms are, and how to request a refund if needed — reducing purchase anxiety significantly.
