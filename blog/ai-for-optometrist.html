What Questions Your AI Employee Can Answer

For an optometry practice, the most common inquiries are repetitive and perfectly suited for AI automation:

  • "Do you do eye exams for children?" — Yes, here's how to book.
  • "How much are contact lens fittings?" — Here's our general pricing range.
  • "Do you carry designer frames?" — Yes, here are our brands.
  • "I need to renew my prescription — do I need a full exam?" — The AI explains your process.
  • "What insurance do you accept?" — It lists your accepted plans or directs them to call.
  • "Can I book online?" — It explains your booking method and collects their info.

None of these require a doctor's judgment. They're administrative questions that an AI employee handles perfectly, freeing your staff for higher-value interactions.
