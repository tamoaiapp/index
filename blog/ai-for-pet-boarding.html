What Your AI Employee Handles for Pet Boarding

  • Availability questions: "Do you have space for two dogs from December 22-27?" — The AI collects the details and explains how to confirm availability.
  • Pricing: "How much is boarding per night for a large dog?" — Clear pricing provided instantly.
  • Vaccination requirements: "What vaccinations does my dog need to board?" — Standard requirements explained clearly.
  • Amenities and care: "Do the dogs get playtime?" "Do you have indoor/outdoor runs?" "Can my dog sleep in a bed?"
  • Special needs: "My dog needs medication twice a day — can you handle that?" — The AI explains your medication administration policy.
  • New client registration: The AI explains what information and documents are needed for first-time boarders.
  • Pick-up and drop-off hours: Instantly answered every time.
