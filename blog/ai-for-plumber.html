Running a plumbing business means you're constantly split between two worlds. In one, you're under sinks, in basements, dealing with systems that require your full attention. In the other, you need to be responsive, professional, and fast on the phone and messaging apps.

Most plumbers handle this badly — not because they don't care, but because it's physically impossible to do both at once. You end up returning calls three hours later to people who have already hired someone else. You lose evenings to quote messages that came in during the day. You miss weekend emergencies because your phone was on silent.

The cost adds up fast. A single missed emergency call that goes to a competitor could be worth $300 to $800. Miss two a week and you're looking at tens of thousands of dollars a year in lost revenue.

How an AI Employee Handles Plumbing Inquiries

TamoWork gives your plumbing business an AI employee that reads every incoming WhatsApp and Instagram message and responds immediately — at any hour, on any day. It's not a phone tree or a scripted menu. It understands natural language and gives real, useful replies.

Here's what it handles specifically for plumbers:

Emergency triage

When someone messages "pipe burst in kitchen," the AI employee recognizes urgency, asks clarifying questions (can they turn off the water main? what's the extent of the leak?), and immediately flags this as an emergency, telling the customer you'll call them within minutes. You get a notification and can call back with full context already gathered.

Availability communication

For non-emergency requests, the AI tells customers your general availability — same day, next day, or scheduled — and asks for their address and a description of the issue. No more vague "I'll call you back" messages that leave customers uncertain.

Quote pre-screening

Before you waste time driving across town for a job that won't pay what you need, the AI can ask about the type of work, property size, and any complications (like access issues or older pipes). You arrive with better information and can quote faster.

Service type clarification

Many customers don't know what they need. "My toilet is making a noise" could be a flapper, a fill valve, or a wax ring. The AI asks the right questions to narrow it down, so you know whether it's a 20-minute fix or a half-day job before you commit time.
