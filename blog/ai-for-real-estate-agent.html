What an AI Employee Does for Real Estate Agents

TamoWork gives your real estate practice an AI employee that handles incoming messages on WhatsApp and Instagram 24/7 — free, running on your own computer, with no monthly fees.

Lead qualification

Not every inquiry is at the same stage. Some people are actively searching with pre-approval in hand. Others are casually browsing without a timeline. Your AI employee can ask key qualification questions — are they buying or selling, what's their timeline, do they have financing in place, what neighborhoods are they interested in — so you can prioritize your callbacks by lead readiness.

Listing inquiries

When someone messages about a specific listing, the AI can provide additional details, answer common questions about the property, explain the offer process, and schedule a viewing — all before you've even seen the message. By the time you follow up, the lead is warm and the viewing is on the calendar.

Neighborhood and price range questions

Buyers often ask general questions before committing to a specific search: "What's the market like in [area]?" or "What can I get for $400K?" Your AI employee can provide general market context, help narrow down search parameters, and guide the prospect toward a consultation with you.

Seller inquiries

Potential sellers often start by asking "what's my home worth?" Your AI employee can explain your valuation process, collect property details for a comparative market analysis, and schedule a listing consultation — converting what would have been a casual inquiry into a real lead on your calendar.
