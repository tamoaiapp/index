AI for Sandwich Shops: Automate Daily Orders

🗓 March 22, 2026⏱ 8 min read

Sandwich shops and delis live and die by the lunch rush. For two hours in the middle of every weekday, you need to move as many orders as possible — quickly, accurately, and without errors. Any friction in the ordering process reduces your throughput. Any unanswered pre-order messages means a customer who went somewhere else.

The shift toward digital ordering — customers placing orders on WhatsApp before arriving for pickup — is a huge opportunity for sandwich shops that embrace it. Customers love the convenience. You love the reduced chaos at the counter. But it only works if the digital ordering experience is fast and seamless. An AI employee makes it so.

What Your Sandwich Shop AI Employee Handles

Today's Menu and Daily Specials

Many sandwich shops rotate daily specials — a different featured sandwich each day, or a soup of the day that changes throughout the week. Your AI employee is updated with today's offerings and shares them immediately with anyone who asks. "Today's special is the Italian Beef on ciabatta with roasted peppers and provolone. We also have French onion soup today."

Pre-Orders for Pickup

The core use case for sandwich shops: a customer sends their order via WhatsApp, the AI employee confirms the details and provides a pickup time estimate, and the order goes to your kitchen. At pickup time, the customer walks in, gives their name, and leaves in under two minutes. Efficient for them, profitable for you.

Customization Handling

Sandwich customization is complex — bread type, protein, cheese, vegetables, condiments, toasted or not, cut preference. Your AI employee guides customers through a structured customization conversation that captures all these preferences clearly. No more deciphering "the usual but without the onions" from a new customer you've never served before.

Corporate and Group Orders

Office catering orders — sandwiches for 15 people for a meeting — are high-value orders that require advance coordination. Your AI employee handles the initial inquiry, collects all order details (items, quantities, dietary requirements, delivery vs pickup, timing), and routes the complete order to you for confirmation. These orders often become repeat business that anchors your weekly revenue.

Setting Up Your Sandwich Shop AI Employee

  1. Document your full menu with customization options. Every bread type, protein, cheese, vegetable, and condiment. This is the foundation of accurate order collection.
  2. Define your daily special update routine. Update TamoWork each morning with today's specials before the lunch rush starts.
  3. Set your pickup time estimates. Standard orders in X minutes, larger orders in Y minutes, corporate orders require Z hours notice.
  4. Build your corporate order intake flow. What information do you need for large group orders? Collect it systematically.
  5. Configure and test the ordering flow. Place a test order yourself, including multiple customizations, and verify the experience is smooth and accurate.
