Sewing shop customers fall into two broad categories: those buying machines and those seeking service, parts, or supplies. Each has its own set of common questions:

Machine buyers ask:

  • What's the best machine for a beginner/quilter/embroiderer?
  • What's the difference between [model A] and [model B]?
  • Do you offer financing on machines?
  • Do you have [brand/model] in stock?
  • Can I trade in my old machine?

Service and supply customers ask:

  • How much does a machine service cost?
  • How long does a repair take?
  • Do you carry parts for [brand/model]?
  • Do you offer sewing classes for beginners?
  • Do you sell presser feet and accessories for [brand]?

Machine service and repair is a consistent revenue stream for sewing shops.
