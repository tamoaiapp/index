Spas face a specific conversion problem: the client journey from discovery to booking tends to be longer and more question-intensive than other beauty businesses. A client considering a facial or a massage package wants to understand exactly what is included, how long it takes, what to expect during the treatment, and whether it is appropriate for their specific concern.

These are not quick one-liner questions — they often involve a back-and-forth that takes time. When a potential client sends a thoughtful question via Instagram DM and gets no reply for six hours, the moment is lost. They have moved on, either to a competitor or to the comfortable inaction of "I'll do it another time."

An AI employee keeps that moment alive. It responds immediately, with enough detail to satisfy the question and enough warmth to keep the client engaged.

Spas receive health-related questions that require care. "Can I get a massage while pregnant?" "I have a skin condition — is a facial safe for me?" These deserve thoughtful, accurate responses — not dismissal, and not overconfident claims.
