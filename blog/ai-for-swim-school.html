How to Set Up TamoWork for Your Swim School

Document your programs in detail

Write out every class you offer: age groups, skill levels, days, times, session lengths, class sizes, instructor certifications, pricing, and enrollment policies. Include information about your pool facility — whether it's heated, indoor or outdoor, and any safety features parents care about. This is your AI employee's complete knowledge base.

Create enrollment pathways

Every conversation should end with a clear next step. Configure your AI employee to always invite parents to book a trial lesson or send an enrollment form link. This transforms passive inquiry responses into active enrollment actions.

Handle capacity and waitlists

Configure your AI employee to know when classes are full and to automatically offer waitlist registration. Parents appreciate being told clearly — and being given an option — rather than receiving silence.

Flag urgent situations

If a parent mentions a medical condition, a child with special needs, or a safety concern, those conversations should be escalated immediately to you. Set clear triggers for these situations while letting routine questions run on autopilot.
