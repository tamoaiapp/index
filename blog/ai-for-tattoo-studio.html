Tattoo inquiries are more complex than most service businesses. Clients want to know about your style, your minimum pricing, your deposit policy, how long the wait is to get an appointment with a specific artist, what the aftercare process looks like, and whether you do touch-ups. For studios with multiple artists, each with different specialties and availability, the complexity multiplies.

Add to that the fact that tattoo clients are often apprehensive first-timers with a lot of questions, and you start to understand why the inbox can become a full-time job. An artist who responds quickly and thoroughly to first-timer questions builds enormous trust before the client ever walks through the door. An artist who takes days to reply loses them entirely.
