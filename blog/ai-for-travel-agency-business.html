The challenge travel agencies face online

Travel agencies receive varied and urgent questions all the time: "Do you have flights to Cancun in July?", "Do I need a visa for Portugal?", "How much is the Orlando package?". Every question left unanswered quickly is a lost sale.

Today, customers who don't get a response within minutes will look elsewhere. Artificial intelligence solves this by responding instantly on Instagram and WhatsApp, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

What AI can do for your travel agency

Answer destination questions: required documentation, best time to visit, tips for each destination.

Inform about available packages: prices, what's included, available dates — all based on what you configured.

Qualify leads: the AI asks customers which destination they want, how many people, and what dates — collecting this info before passing to the human agent.

Handle weekend inquiries: when customers have time to plan their trip, the AI is available to chat — even when you're not.
