Common Questions Your AI Employee Can Handle

Subjects and Grade Levels

Parents want to know if you're the right fit before investing time in a conversation. Your AI employee can clearly list every subject you teach, the grade levels you work with, and any specializations — such as exam prep, learning differences support, or advanced placement courses.

Pricing and Session Packages

Transparent pricing builds immediate trust. Your AI employee can share your hourly rate, any discounted monthly packages, group session pricing, and how payment works. This removes the awkward pricing dance and moves prospects faster toward a decision.

Scheduling and Availability

One of the most common questions tutors receive is about schedule compatibility. Your AI employee can share your available days and times, explain how far in advance sessions must be booked, and describe your cancellation policy — helping parents quickly determine if your schedule works for their family.

Teaching Methodology

Parents often want to understand your approach before committing. Your AI employee can explain whether you follow the school curriculum, use diagnostic assessments, focus on exam technique, or take a more holistic learning approach. This professional transparency sets you apart from tutors who provide no context about how they work.
