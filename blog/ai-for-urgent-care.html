AI for Urgent Care Centers: Never Miss a Patient Again

AI automation fills this gap perfectly. An AI employee that responds instantly to social media and messaging inquiries ensures that every potential patient gets immediate information — without adding to your front desk's workload.

What an AI Employee Answers for Urgent Care

  • Hours and holiday availability: "Are you open on Easter Sunday?" — Answered instantly with your actual hours.
  • Services: "Do you treat fractures?" "Do you have X-ray on-site?" "Can you stitch a cut?"
  • Wait time questions: "How long is the wait right now?" — The AI can direct them to your wait time tool or provide general information.
  • Insurance: "Do you accept United Healthcare?" — Lists your accepted plans.
  • Occupational health: "Do you do workers' comp cases?" "Do you offer drug testing for employers?"
  • COVID and testing: "Do you offer rapid COVID tests?" "Do you do travel PCR tests?"
  • Location: "Which location is closest to [neighborhood]?" — The AI explains your locations.
