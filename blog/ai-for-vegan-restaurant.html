Setting Up Your Vegan Restaurant AI Employee

  1. Build your complete ingredient database. Every dish, every ingredient, every allergen. Include sourcing information where relevant.
  2. Write your kitchen practices documentation. How do you handle cross-contamination? What does "vegan" mean in your kitchen specifically?
  3. Create ingredient glossary entries. For unfamiliar ingredients, write brief, friendly explanations that help new customers feel comfortable.
  4. Document your delivery setup and any event booking information.
  5. Configure your community voice. Set the tone for responses — enthusiastic, values-aligned, knowledgeable — so the AI reflects your restaurant's personality.
