The waxing salon that responds to that first-timer with warmth, professionalism, and complete information wins a client who could return monthly for years. The salon that takes 18 hours to reply gets a client who decides it is too much effort and shaves instead.

Speed and quality of that first response is disproportionately important in this industry. An AI employee makes sure every first-timer gets a response that feels personal and professional — immediately.

What an AI Employee Handles for Waxing Salons

TamoWork is a free tool that runs on your computer and connects to your Instagram account. It uses a local AI model (LLaMA via Ollama) to automatically reply to every DM and comment — no subscription, no external server, no API key required.

For waxing salons, the AI employee handles:

  • Service menu — Eyebrow waxing, lip, chin, full face, underarm, arms, legs, Brazilian, bikini, back — whatever you offer, described clearly and professionally.
  • Pricing — Individual service prices and any bundled or repeat-client discounts you offer.
  • Hair length requirements — "For best results, hair should be at least a quarter inch long — about 2–3 weeks of growth after shaving." This single piece of information prevents the most common first-timer mistake.
  • Pre-care instructions — Exfoliating beforehand, avoiding certain products, what to wear, whether to take a painkiller in advance.
  • Post-care instructions — What to avoid for 24–48 hours, how to prevent ingrown hairs, when they can use certain skincare products.
  • Pain expectations — Honest, reassuring information about what clients can expect, especially for sensitive areas.
  • Booking process — How to schedule, your cancellation policy, whether you take same-day bookings.
