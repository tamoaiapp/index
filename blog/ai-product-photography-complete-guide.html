AI Product Photography: Complete Guide for Sellers

TamoWork · 2026-04-13

Professional product photography used to cost $30-80 per shot. AI has changed that completely. Here is everything you need to know about using AI for product photos.

What AI product photography actually does

AI tools like TamoWork take your existing product photos and transform them: remove backgrounds, fix lighting, add professional backdrops, and enhance image quality — all automatically, in seconds.

How it compares to traditional photography

Traditional studio: $500-2000 per session, 3-7 day turnaround, limited to what you can physically set up. AI: fixed monthly cost, results in under a minute, unlimited creative options for backgrounds and styles.

Best products for AI photography

AI works best with solid products: clothing, electronics, cosmetics, bags, shoes, home goods. Products with transparent or very complex surfaces (glass bottles, intricate jewelry) may require more processing.

What you need to get started

Just your phone camera and decent lighting. Take the photo with a plain background (white paper works fine). Upload to TamoWork. AI does the rest. No Photoshop skills required.

ROI: does it actually pay off

A Shopify study found listings with professional photos have 30% higher conversion rates. At $49/month for unlimited AI processing vs $500+ for a single photoshoot — the math is obvious for any seller with more than 10 products.
