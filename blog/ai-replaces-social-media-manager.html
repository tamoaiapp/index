What a Social Media Manager Actually Does for DMs

When small businesses hire social media managers for Instagram, the primary customer service tasks usually fall into a few categories:

  • Responding to DMs asking about products, services, and pricing

  • Routing complex issues to the business owner

Look at that list carefully. The first four items are almost entirely information-based tasks — answering the same questions that customers ask every day. These are exactly the tasks an AI employee handles automatically and without cost.
