Where Virtual Assistants Still Win

VAs have genuine advantages for complex, nuanced tasks. If a customer has a very unusual complaint, is emotionally distressed, or requires a creative solution that is not covered in your standard information, a human VA can navigate that better than any AI.

VAs are also better for tasks that go beyond customer service — scheduling, research, content creation, and other cognitive tasks that require human creativity and judgment.
