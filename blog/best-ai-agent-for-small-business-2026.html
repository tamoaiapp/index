What Makes an AI Agent "Best" for Small Businesses?

The criteria that matter most for small businesses differ from enterprise requirements. For small business owners, the evaluation should focus on:

  • Cost: Ongoing monthly cost, including any API fees
  • Ease of setup: Time required to get operational, technical knowledge needed
  • Channel coverage: Which platforms it connects to (Instagram, WhatsApp, etc.)
  • Response quality: How well it handles real customer questions
  • Privacy: What happens to your customer data
  • Reliability: Uptime, consistency, no surprise outages
