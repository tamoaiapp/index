  • Reply style matters: Train your AI employee to match your natural voice. If you're casual with customers, reflect that in your training notes.
  • Handle sensitive comments manually: Configure TamoWork to flag certain comment types (complaints, negative reviews) for your personal attention instead of auto-replying.
  • Promote your latest offer: Update your AI's training info whenever you have a new promotion so it mentions it naturally in replies.
