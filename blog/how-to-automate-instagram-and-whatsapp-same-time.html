Step by Step: How to Do It

Step 1

Download and Install TamoWork

Go to tamowork.com and download the free installer. One installation covers both Instagram and WhatsApp — no need to install two separate tools. Works on Windows with 4GB+ RAM.

Step 2

Set Up the Instagram Employee First

Open TamoWork and start with the Instagram Employee. Connect your Instagram account, fill in your business training data, and configure your reply preferences for DMs and comments. Complete this setup and run a quick test before moving to WhatsApp.

Step 3

Set Up the WhatsApp Employee

Back on the TamoWork dashboard, click "WhatsApp Employee." A QR code will appear. Open WhatsApp on your phone, go to Linked Devices, and scan the QR code to connect your WhatsApp account to TamoWork. The connection takes about 30 seconds.

Step 4

Share Training Data Across Both Employees

If your business information is the same for both channels (which it usually is), you can copy your Instagram training data into the WhatsApp training form. This saves time and ensures consistent replies across both platforms. Customize the tone if needed — WhatsApp conversations tend to be slightly more casual.

Step 5

Configure Channel-Specific Settings

Instagram and WhatsApp have different interaction patterns. For Instagram, configure comment reply rules alongside DM settings. For WhatsApp, you might want to set group message handling rules separately from individual message rules. TamoWork handles both configurations independently.

Step 6

Test Both Channels Simultaneously

Using test accounts, send messages to both your Instagram DMs and your WhatsApp at the same time. Watch TamoWork handle both in parallel. Make sure the responses on each platform match your expectations. Refine training info as needed.

Step 7

Activate Both and Keep TamoWork Running

Enable both the Instagram Employee and the WhatsApp Employee. Both will run concurrently in the background. Your computer is now a full-coverage customer service hub — handling two major communication channels 24/7 without any additional staff or monthly fees.

Important Tips

  • One training session, two employees: While you can customize each employee independently, starting with the same core business information for both saves a lot of setup time.
  • Monitor both dashboards weekly: Spend 10 minutes each week reviewing conversations from both channels. This helps you identify patterns and improve your training data.
  • Consistent branding matters: Make sure the tone and information your AI employee uses on Instagram matches what it says on WhatsApp. Inconsistency confuses customers.
  • Resource usage: Running both employees simultaneously is light on system resources. Even a basic 4GB RAM Windows machine handles it without issue.
