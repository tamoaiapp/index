Step-by-Step: Automating Your Instagram DM Replies

Step 1

Download and Install TamoWork

Go to tamowork.com and download the installer for your operating system. Run the installer — it takes about two minutes. TamoWork comes bundled with everything it needs, including the local AI model. You do not need to install anything else separately.

Step 2

Connect Your Instagram Account

Open TamoWork and navigate to the Instagram section. Click "Connect Instagram" and follow the login flow. TamoWork uses Instagram's official API connection — you authorize it the same way you would authorize any app. Your password never touches TamoWork's servers because everything runs locally.

Step 3

Set Up Your Business Profile

In the setup wizard, describe your business in plain language. For example: "I sell handmade jewelry. My pieces are made to order and ship in 5–7 business days. Prices range from $15 to $120." The AI uses this information to craft accurate, helpful replies. The more detail you add, the better the answers will be.

Step 4

Configure Your Response Style

Choose the tone you want your AI employee to use. Options range from very friendly and casual to professional and concise. You can also add specific phrases you always use, or phrases to avoid. This makes the replies feel like they genuinely come from your brand.

Step 5

Add Your FAQ Answers

List the questions you get asked most often and write the answers. Common examples: shipping time, return policy, payment methods, product sizes, and pricing. TamoWork's AI will use these as the primary source of truth. If a customer asks something covered in your FAQ, the answer will be fast and accurate.

Step 6

Turn On DM Automation

Toggle the "Automate DMs" switch to ON. From this point, whenever a new DM arrives on your Instagram, TamoWork reads it, understands the intent, generates a relevant reply based on your business profile and FAQ, and sends it automatically — all within seconds.

Step 7

Review and Refine

Check the reply log regularly for the first few days. You will see every message that came in and every reply that was sent. If any reply was not quite right, update your business profile or FAQ with more detail. Most users find that after 3–5 refinements, the quality is excellent.

Tips to Get the Best Results

  • Be specific in your business description. Vague descriptions produce generic replies. If you sell cakes, say what flavors, how many days in advance orders need to be placed, and what cities you deliver to.
  • Update your FAQ as new questions come in. When you see a question the AI did not handle perfectly, add it to the FAQ with your preferred answer.
  • Use the review log daily at first. Treat it like inbox monitoring — just a quick 2-minute scan to make sure everything looks right.
  • Do not automate everything from day one. Start with DMs only, then add comment replies once you are comfortable with how the AI handles your messages.
  • Keep your business info current. If your prices change, update TamoWork immediately. Outdated information will lead to customer confusion.

What Happens When the AI Does Not Know the Answer?

TamoWork is designed to be honest. If a customer asks something that is not in your business profile or FAQ — like a very specific custom order request — the AI will acknowledge the message warmly and let the customer know you will follow up personally. This prevents the AI from making up information while still ensuring the customer feels heard immediately.
