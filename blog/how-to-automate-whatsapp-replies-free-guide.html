Step-by-Step: Automating Your WhatsApp Replies

Step 1

Switch to WhatsApp Business

Download the free WhatsApp Business app and migrate your existing number to it. WhatsApp Business gives you a business profile and provides the API hooks that TamoWork uses to monitor and respond to messages. The migration takes about 5 minutes and preserves all your existing chats.

Step 2

Download and Install TamoWork

Go to tamowork.com and download the installer for your operating system. Run the installer — it takes about 3 minutes. TamoWork installs a local AI model on your machine. No external accounts, no API keys, no monthly fees. The AI runs entirely on your computer.

Step 3

Connect Your WhatsApp Business Account

Open TamoWork and navigate to Accounts — WhatsApp — Connect. You will see a QR code. Open WhatsApp Business on your phone, go to Settings — Linked Devices — Link a Device, and scan the QR code. TamoWork is now linked and will receive all incoming messages automatically.

Step 4

Create Your Business Profile

In TamoWork, go to AI Settings — Business Profile and write a detailed description of your business. Include everything a customer might ask: what you sell, your prices, how to order, payment methods, delivery areas and times, and your return policy. The more complete this is, the better your automated replies will be.

Step 5

Build Your FAQ Library

Go to AI Settings — FAQ and add your most common customer questions with your preferred answers. Add 15-25 entries to start. Common WhatsApp FAQ entries: "Do you deliver?", "What's the minimum order?", "Can I pay on delivery?", "How do I track my order?", "Do you offer discounts?"

Step 6

Configure Auto-Reply Settings

In WhatsApp Settings — Automation, enable auto-reply for new messages. Set a response delay of 1-3 minutes. Configure which message types to handle automatically and which to escalate to you personally for review.

Step 7

Set Up Your Escalation Rules

Some WhatsApp messages need personal attention. Configure TamoWork to flag and notify you about: complaint messages, messages from VIP customers, messages containing words like "urgent" or "problem," and any message where the AI's confidence level is low. These go into your personal review queue.

Step 8

Test Your Setup Before Going Live

Use Test Mode. Have a friend send a few test messages to your WhatsApp covering common scenarios: a price inquiry, a delivery question, and a custom order request. Review what TamoWork would have replied. Adjust your FAQ and business profile until the replies match what you would write yourself.

Tips for the Best WhatsApp Automation Results

  • Keep your business profile updated. If your prices or stock changes, update TamoWork immediately.
  • Use a 1-2 minute delay. Instant replies on WhatsApp can feel unusual. A slight delay reads as more natural.
  • Do not automate group chats. Keep automation limited to direct messages from individual customers.
  • Review your reply log daily for the first two weeks, then weekly once the system is well-tuned.
