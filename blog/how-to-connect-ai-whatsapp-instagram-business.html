Step by Step: How to Do It

Step 1

Download and Install TamoWork

Go to tamowork.com and download the free Windows installer. One installation covers both Instagram and WhatsApp. Run the installer — it takes about 3 minutes. Open TamoWork from the desktop shortcut when done.

Step 2

Connect Instagram First

From the TamoWork dashboard, click "Instagram Employee." Follow the setup wizard to connect your Instagram account via the secure login window. Complete the business training form for Instagram. Configure your DM and comment reply preferences. Test with a sample message.

Step 3

Connect WhatsApp Second

Return to the main dashboard and click "WhatsApp Employee." A QR code appears. Open WhatsApp on your phone > Settings > Linked Devices > Link a Device. Scan the QR code. WhatsApp is now connected to TamoWork alongside Instagram.

Step 4

Create a Unified Business Training Document

For consistent AI performance across both channels, create a single comprehensive training document with all your business information. Then apply this same core data to both the Instagram Employee and the WhatsApp Employee. This ensures customers get consistent information regardless of which channel they use.

Step 5

Customize Each Channel's Tone

While the core information should be consistent, you can adjust the tone for each platform. Instagram conversations — especially in comments — may benefit from a slightly more casual, public-facing tone. WhatsApp tends toward more direct, personal conversation. Configure each employee's communication style accordingly.

Step 6

Test Both Channels Simultaneously

Using secondary accounts, send test messages to both your Instagram DMs and your WhatsApp at the same time. Verify that TamoWork handles both concurrently without delays. Check that replies on both channels are accurate and appropriately toned. Adjust training as needed.

Step 7

Enable Both and Monitor the Combined Dashboard

Activate both employees from the TamoWork dashboard. Both run simultaneously in the background. From TamoWork's unified view, you can see conversations from both channels in one place — making your daily review fast and efficient even with two active channels.
