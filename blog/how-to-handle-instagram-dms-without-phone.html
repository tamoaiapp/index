Step-by-Step Setup

Step 1

Install TamoWork on Your PC or Mac

Download TamoWork from tamowork.com. The installer is simple and takes about 2 minutes to complete. TamoWork runs quietly in the background on your computer — you do not need to keep any window open. Once it is running, it monitors and responds to your Instagram DMs automatically.

Step 2

Connect Your Instagram Account

Open TamoWork and navigate to Accounts → Instagram → Connect. Authorize the app through Instagram's secure login. TamoWork will begin syncing your DM inbox immediately after authorization. From this point, your computer is handling the monitoring — not your phone.

Step 3

Write Your Business Profile Once

In AI Settings → Business Profile, describe your business thoroughly. Products, prices, order process, shipping, return policy, location if relevant. Write it the way you would explain your business to a new employee on their first day. This becomes the knowledge base your AI employee uses to answer questions.

Step 4

Set Up Your Priority Filters

Not all DMs are equal. Configure TamoWork to send you a desktop notification (not a phone alert) only for messages that match certain criteria: large orders, complaints, specific keywords like "urgent" or "problem," or messages from repeat customers. Everything else gets handled by the AI automatically.

Step 5

Turn Off Instagram Notifications on Your Phone

This is the liberating step. Go to your phone's settings and disable Instagram DM notifications. You no longer need them — TamoWork is handling the DMs on your computer. If something truly urgent comes in, you set up desktop alerts for that. Your phone becomes a content-creation device, not an inbox you have to babysit.

Step 6

Schedule Your Daily DM Review

Instead of reacting to every DM as it arrives, schedule a 10-minute DM review once or twice a day. Open TamoWork's dashboard, review the day's conversations, respond personally to any that need your attention, and close the laptop. That is it. Your Instagram DMs are now on your schedule, not Instagram's.

Step 7

Refine Your AI Employee Over Time

During your daily reviews, note any replies that were not quite right. Update your FAQ and business profile with better answers. After a week of refinement, most TamoWork users find that 90%+ of their DMs are handled perfectly without their involvement. The AI gets better the more you teach it.
