Step-by-Step: Weekend Instagram Coverage

Step 1

Install TamoWork and Set Up Your Account

Download and install TamoWork from tamowork.com. Connect your Instagram Business account. Configure TamoWork to run as a startup service on your computer — this means it starts automatically whenever your computer turns on, including after restarts. This ensures you do not have to manually launch it every Friday before the weekend.

Step 2

Build Your Weekend-Ready Business Profile

Write a thorough business profile in TamoWork's AI Settings. Include everything a customer might need to know over a weekend when you are not available to step in: your full product range, pricing, how to order, payment options, shipping and delivery times, and your return policy. Think of it as writing the complete guide your stand-in employee would need for a solo weekend shift.

Step 3

Create Weekend-Specific FAQ Entries

Weekends often bring questions that are slightly different from weekday questions. Add FAQ entries for: "Are you open on weekends?" (Yes — messages are answered 24/7), "Can I get my order before Monday?" (be specific about your actual capabilities), and "I sent a message yesterday and did not hear back" (your AI is now handling all messages around the clock). Addressing these weekend-specific scenarios helps set proper expectations.

Step 4

Configure a "Weekend Mode" Response Style

Some business owners like their weekend replies to be slightly warmer and more casual — a tone that acknowledges it is the weekend and still feels friendly. In TamoWork's communication settings, you can add style notes like "feel free to be a little more casual and warm on weekend messages." The AI picks up on this nuance.

Step 5

Set Up an Escalation Queue for Monday

For any messages the AI cannot handle over the weekend — unusual complaints, complex custom requests — set them up to go into an escalation queue that you review first thing Monday morning. In TamoWork's escalation settings, configure these messages to also send you an email or desktop notification so they are waiting for you when you start your week, not buried in your inbox.

Step 6

Schedule Your Friday Prep Check

Every Friday afternoon, spend 10 minutes doing a quick pre-weekend check: Is TamoWork running? Is the computer set to stay on? Is your business profile up to date (did you launch any new products or run any promotions this week that the AI should know about)? This Friday ritual ensures you head into the weekend confident that your AI employee is ready.

Step 7

Review Monday Morning and Capture Orders

Start every Monday with a 10-minute TamoWork review. How many messages came in over the weekend? How many were handled successfully? Any orders to fulfill? Any escalated messages needing personal replies? This Monday morning habit keeps you in touch with what happened without requiring you to monitor your phone all weekend.
