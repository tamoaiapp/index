How to Handle WhatsApp Messages While You Sleep

March 22, 2026  ·  6 min read

You close WhatsApp at 11 PM, finally put down your phone, and go to bed. By morning, there are 12 new messages. Some are simple questions. Some are orders that could have been placed overnight. Some are from customers who, by now, have already moved on. This is the reality for most small business owners using WhatsApp.

You deserve to sleep. And your business deserves to keep running while you do. This guide shows you how to set up a free AI employee that handles your WhatsApp overnight — so you wake up to served customers instead of a backlog of unanswered messages.

Why You Need This

Overnight messages represent real opportunity. Many customers shop during evenings and nights — after work, during commutes, or while relaxing before bed. If your WhatsApp shows no response until 9 AM the next day, you've given competitors an 8-hour head start on every one of those potential customers.

TamoWork solves this by running your WhatsApp AI employee continuously on your PC — through the night, through weekends, through holidays. It replies to every message with accurate business information so customers get what they need immediately, and you can review everything fresh in the morning.

Step by Step: How to Do It

Step 1

Download and Install TamoWork

Go to tamowork.com on your PC. Download the free installer and run it. The installation takes about 3 minutes. No account required. A desktop shortcut appears when done.

Step 2

Connect Your WhatsApp

Open TamoWork and select "WhatsApp Employee." A QR code appears on screen. On your phone, open WhatsApp > Settings > Linked Devices > Link a Device. Scan the QR code. Your WhatsApp is now connected to TamoWork for automated replies.

Step 3

Train the AI with Your Business Details

Fill in the training form thoroughly. For overnight automation in particular, include complete information about: all your products and prices, every delivery option available, your return policy, and answers to your most common questions. The overnight AI employee needs to be self-sufficient — it can't ask you for clarification at 2 AM.

Step 4

Configure Overnight-Specific Messaging

Set up a tone and style appropriate for after-hours messages. Something like: warm and friendly, provide full information immediately, note that while replies are automatic, you personally review everything each morning. This balances speed with honesty and sets good customer expectations.

Step 5

Adjust Your Windows Power Settings

For TamoWork to run overnight, your PC needs to stay on. Open Windows Power Settings and set "Sleep" to "Never" or to a long time period (like 4 hours). You can keep the monitor turning off to save power — TamoWork runs without the screen needing to be on.

Step 6

Add TamoWork to Windows Startup

Make sure TamoWork starts automatically after any restart. Press Win+R, type "shell:startup," and press Enter. Drag a shortcut to TamoWork into that folder. Now even if the PC restarts overnight, TamoWork will relaunch automatically.

Step 7

Test Overnight and Review in the Morning

Activate TamoWork before bed. In the morning, check the overnight conversations. See how many customers were served, how many orders were potentially moved forward, and whether any responses need improvement. Refine your training data based on what you find. After a week or two, you'll have an overnight AI employee you can trust completely.

Important Tips

  • Keep the PC plugged in: Make sure your computer is connected to power overnight so it doesn't run out of battery if it's a laptop.
  • Use a wired internet connection: WiFi can occasionally disconnect overnight. A wired Ethernet connection is more reliable for continuous WhatsApp automation.
  • Morning review takes 5 minutes: With TamoWork handling the replies, your morning WhatsApp review becomes a quick check rather than a stressful catch-up session. Review the important conversations and move on with your day.
