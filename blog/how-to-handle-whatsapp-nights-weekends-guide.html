Step-by-Step: Weekend and Night WhatsApp Coverage

Step 1

Choose Your Always-On Computer

TamoWork needs a computer that stays running for continuous coverage. This can be your main computer (configured to never sleep) or a dedicated secondary machine — even an old laptop works perfectly. The computer needs a stable internet connection and ideally a power adapter so it never runs out of battery. Set up your power/sleep settings so the machine stays active around the clock.

Step 2

Install TamoWork as a Startup Service

Download and install TamoWork from tamowork.com. During installation, or in the app's settings afterward, configure TamoWork to start automatically when the computer boots. This way, even after a power outage or unexpected restart, TamoWork comes back online automatically without you needing to intervene.

Step 3

Connect Your WhatsApp Business Account

Open TamoWork and navigate to Accounts — WhatsApp — Connect. Scan the QR code with WhatsApp Business (Settings — Linked Devices — Link a Device). Once linked, TamoWork monitors your WhatsApp continuously. The phone just needs to be connected to the internet — it does not need to be near the computer or even turned on for TamoWork to function.

Step 4

Create a Comprehensive Business Profile for Unsupervised Operation

On nights and weekends, TamoWork operates without you being available as a backup. This means your business profile and FAQ need to be complete enough to handle the full range of routine questions independently. Write your business profile as if you are briefing someone who will be completely alone managing your WhatsApp for 72 hours straight. Every detail matters.

Step 5

Set Your "Off Hours" Mode

In TamoWork's settings, define your off hours and configure the automation behavior for those periods. During off hours: full auto-reply, no delay needed (or minimal delay), and all escalated messages go to a queue rather than a live notification (unless truly urgent). During business hours: you may prefer a hybrid approach where you handle messages personally and TamoWork only steps in after a few minutes.

Step 6

Define Your Urgent Message Protocol

Even on weekends, some messages are genuinely urgent. A regular customer with a broken product they need replaced immediately before a big event. A catering order with an error that will affect tomorrow's delivery. Configure TamoWork to send a special notification (a different notification sound, or a text to a secondary number) for messages that contain genuinely urgent signals. Everything else gets handled by the AI without interrupting your weekend.

Step 7

Build Your Monday/Post-Weekend Review Routine

Every Monday morning (and every morning after any extended period of AI-only coverage), start with a TamoWork review. Look at what happened, check that everything was handled appropriately, and address any queued escalated messages. This 10-minute routine keeps you fully informed and in control without requiring you to monitor WhatsApp constantly during your off hours.
