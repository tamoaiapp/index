Step-by-Step: After-Hours WhatsApp Coverage

Step 1

Install TamoWork and Configure for Overnight Running

Download and install TamoWork from tamowork.com. During setup, enable the option to run TamoWork as a startup service — this ensures it starts automatically when your computer turns on and continues running without needing a user logged in. Also configure your computer's power settings to prevent sleep mode so TamoWork runs through the night uninterrupted.

Step 2

Connect Your WhatsApp Business Account

Link your WhatsApp Business account to TamoWork using the QR code scan method. Once linked, TamoWork receives incoming messages regardless of whether your phone is in your pocket or switched off. The connection is maintained through your computer's internet connection, not your phone's.

Step 3

Define Your Business Hours and After-Hours Behavior

In TamoWork's Business Hours Settings, define your actual working hours (for example, Monday-Friday 9am-6pm, Saturday 10am-2pm). Then configure different automation behavior for each time period: during business hours, you might prefer to reply personally to most messages; outside business hours, full auto-reply kicks in. This gives you a hybrid approach that maximizes quality during the day and coverage at night.

Step 4

Write an After-Hours Business Profile

Go to AI Settings — Business Profile and make sure your profile includes everything needed for complete after-hours coverage. Pay special attention to: your ordering process (so an overnight customer can place an order without needing to wait for you), your pricing (so there are no surprises), and your estimated response time for anything that needs personal attention the next day.

Step 5

Add After-Hours Specific FAQ Entries

Create FAQ entries that address after-hours scenarios specifically. Examples: "Are you available now?" → "Our team is currently unavailable but this message has been received and I can answer your question right now. [then answer the question]." "When can I expect a reply from someone?" → "For anything this message can't fully address, a team member will follow up by [specific time]." Setting clear expectations prevents frustration.

Step 6

Set Up Your Morning Follow-Up Workflow

Configure TamoWork to compile a morning summary of overnight messages. This summary should include: all conversations that happened, which ones were resolved by the AI, and which ones need your personal follow-up. Set a reminder on your phone to check TamoWork first thing every morning and handle the personal follow-up queue before you start other work.

Step 7

Enable Urgent Message Alerts

Even after hours, some messages need immediate attention — a regular customer with a serious problem, an urgent order for a special event happening tomorrow. Configure TamoWork to send you a phone notification (separate from WhatsApp) when a message contains certain keywords like "urgent," "emergency," or "tomorrow." This gives you an escape hatch for genuinely time-sensitive situations without making you monitor your phone constantly.
