Step-by-Step: Zero Missed WhatsApp Messages

Step 1

Set Up WhatsApp Business on Your Phone

If you are still using the regular WhatsApp app, download WhatsApp Business and migrate your number. This is free. WhatsApp Business offers business profile features and the API connection that TamoWork uses. Your existing contacts and chat history transfer over automatically.

Step 2

Install TamoWork on Your Computer

Go to tamowork.com and download TamoWork for your operating system. Install it — about 3 minutes from download to ready. TamoWork runs quietly in the background, monitoring your WhatsApp for new messages without requiring you to keep any window open. It starts automatically when your computer starts.

Step 3

Link WhatsApp to TamoWork via QR Code

Open TamoWork and go to Accounts — WhatsApp — Connect. Scan the QR code shown in TamoWork using your WhatsApp Business app (Settings — Linked Devices — Link a Device). This is the same process as linking WhatsApp Web. Once linked, TamoWork receives every incoming message in real time.

Step 4

Write a Complete Business Profile

In TamoWork's AI Settings — Business Profile, write a thorough description of your business. Think of every question a customer might ask and make sure the answer is somewhere in this description. Include your product range, pricing, how to order, payment methods you accept, delivery areas, and return policy. This becomes your AI employee's knowledge base.

Step 5

Add Your 20 Most Common Questions

Go to FAQ in TamoWork and add your most frequently asked WhatsApp questions. Think about the last 50 messages you received — what were the most common topics? Add each one with your ideal complete answer. Aim for at least 20 FAQ entries before going live. Each one you add directly improves reply accuracy.

Step 6

Configure Immediate Response Activation

In WhatsApp Automation Settings, enable auto-reply and set your response delay. For maximum coverage, set the delay to 1-2 minutes. This ensures every new WhatsApp message receives a relevant, helpful reply within 2 minutes — even if you are sleeping, driving, or serving another customer in person.

Step 7

Keep Your Computer On for 24/7 Coverage

TamoWork runs as long as your computer is on and connected to the internet. For true 24/7 coverage, configure your computer to never sleep (Power Settings on Windows; System Settings — Battery on Mac). Many users keep an old laptop running specifically for this purpose — it uses very little power and provides complete overnight and weekend coverage.
