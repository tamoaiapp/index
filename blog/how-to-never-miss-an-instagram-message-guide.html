Step-by-Step Setup

Step 1

Install TamoWork on Your Computer

Download TamoWork from tamowork.com and run the installer. The setup takes about two minutes. Everything runs on your PC — there is no cloud service involved. Your data stays private on your own machine.

Step 2

Link Your Instagram Account

Inside TamoWork, click Add Account under the Instagram section. Log in with your Instagram credentials through the secure OAuth flow. TamoWork connects to your account using Instagram's official API and starts monitoring for new messages immediately after authorization.

Step 3

Write a Clear Business Description

This is the most important step. Go to Business Profile and describe exactly what you sell, your prices (or price range), how customers can order, your delivery areas, and your hours. The clearer this description, the more accurately your AI employee will answer questions. Think of it as writing instructions for a new hire on their first day.

Step 4

Create Your FAQ Database

List every question you have ever received more than twice. Common ones include: "Do you ship to [city]?", "What's the price?", "How long does delivery take?", "Can I pay with card?", "Do you do custom orders?". Add your exact preferred answer for each. Your AI employee will use these answers word-for-word when those questions come up.

Step 5

Enable Message Monitoring

In the dashboard, enable DM Auto-Reply and Comment Monitoring. Set the response delay to your preference — anywhere from instant (0 seconds) to a few minutes. Many business owners use a 1–2 minute delay to keep replies feeling natural rather than robotic.

Step 6

Set Up Escalation Rules

Tell TamoWork which types of messages should send you a notification for personal follow-up. For example: messages about complaints, messages that mention a competitor, or messages requesting a custom quote over a certain value. You stay in control of the important conversations while the AI handles the routine ones.

Step 7

Keep TamoWork Running in the Background

TamoWork runs quietly in the system tray on Windows or in the menu bar on Mac. As long as your computer is on, it monitors your Instagram and replies instantly. Many users set their computer to never sleep so TamoWork works overnight. Alternatively, even 12 hours of coverage per day dramatically improves response rates.

Pro Tips for Zero Missed Messages

  • Review the reply log every morning. A 5-minute review tells you exactly what happened overnight and lets you catch anything that needs follow-up.
  • Update your business info weekly. As your offers, prices, or policies change, keep TamoWork's business profile current.
  • Use the "flag for review" feature to mark messages the AI was unsure about — then use those as opportunities to improve your FAQ.
  • Test the system by having a friend send you a few test DMs with typical customer questions before going live.
