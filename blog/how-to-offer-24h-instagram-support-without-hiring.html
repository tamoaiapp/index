Set Up the Instagram Employee

Open TamoWork and select "Instagram Employee." Connect your business Instagram account via the secure login window. The initial setup wizard will guide you through everything you need to configure.

Step 3

Build a Comprehensive Business Knowledge Base

For 24/7 support to work effectively, your AI employee needs thorough training. Go beyond basic product info: include your return policy, shipping options and timelines, warranty information, payment methods accepted, and answers to your most frequently asked questions. The goal is for the AI to handle 90%+ of customer inquiries without your involvement.

Configure After-Hours Messaging

Set up a special tone for messages received outside your working hours. For example, you might want the AI to acknowledge it's outside business hours while still providing full product information and taking customer details for follow-up. This manages expectations while keeping customers engaged.

Step 5

Enable Comment Monitoring

Don't just automate DMs — enable comment replies too. Customers often ask questions in comments on your posts, especially after seeing an ad or a promoted post. Having those comments answered quickly, even at 2 AM, dramatically improves your reputation and conversion rate.

Step 6

Add TamoWork to Windows Startup

To ensure true 24/7 coverage, add TamoWork to your Windows startup programs. This way, every time your computer boots up, TamoWork starts automatically. You'll never accidentally leave customers unattended because you forgot to open the app.

Step 7

Review Weekly and Improve

Once per week, spend 10–15 minutes reviewing the conversations your AI employee had. Identify any gaps — questions it couldn't fully answer or situations it handled poorly. Update your training information to fill those gaps. Over time, your 24/7 support gets better and better.

Important Tips

  • Don't hide that you use AI: Being transparent that replies may be automated, while still being helpful and fast, actually builds trust with modern customers.
