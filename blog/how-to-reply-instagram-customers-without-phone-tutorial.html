Use a secondary Instagram account to send a few test messages — pricing questions, availability inquiries, and general hellos. Read the AI's responses. If anything seems off, update your training information and test again.

Activate and Put Down Your Phone

Click "Activate" in TamoWork and minimize it to the system tray. From this point, your AI employee handles all incoming Instagram messages. You can put your phone down knowing every customer is being taken care of.
