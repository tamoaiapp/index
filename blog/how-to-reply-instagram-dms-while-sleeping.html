Step-by-Step: Automating Overnight Instagram Replies

Step 1

Choose and Set Up Your Overnight Computer

TamoWork runs on any Windows, Mac, or Linux machine. It does not need a powerful computer — even an older laptop with 4GB of RAM works fine. Choose the computer you will leave on overnight, configure its power settings to never sleep, and this becomes your dedicated overnight customer service station. Many users repurpose an old laptop specifically for this.

Step 2

Install TamoWork

Download TamoWork from tamowork.com and run the installer on your chosen computer. TamoWork installs as a background service — it does not need an open window to run. Once started, it will continue monitoring and replying even when you close the app's interface. The computer just needs to stay on and connected to the internet.

Step 3

Connect Your Instagram Account

In TamoWork, go to Accounts → Instagram → Add Account and authorize your business Instagram. Make sure to enable the Always Active toggle so TamoWork never pauses between sessions. Test the connection by checking that TamoWork shows your recent DMs in the dashboard.

Step 4

Write a Thorough Business Profile for Overnight Coverage

When you are awake, you can jump into a conversation if the AI gets something wrong. Overnight, that safety net is not there. This means your business profile needs to be extremely complete. Write detailed answers to every question you can imagine someone asking: every product, every price, every variant, every shipping option, your refund policy, how to pay, and where to find your catalog. Leave nothing out.

Step 5

Create a Complete FAQ for Overnight Queries

Your FAQ is the AI's fallback resource. Add at least 20 entries covering your most common questions. Include edge cases you have seen before. The more complete this is, the fewer overnight conversations will need your follow-up in the morning. Think of it as writing a complete manual for a new overnight employee.

Step 6

Configure a Gentle "I'll Follow Up" Response for Complex Issues

For messages the AI cannot handle confidently — detailed custom orders, complaints, unusual requests — configure TamoWork to acknowledge the message and promise a personal follow-up: "Thanks for reaching out! This looks like something I want to personally help you with. I'll be back with you first thing tomorrow morning." This keeps the customer warm without the AI making promises it can't keep.

Step 7

Set Up Your Morning Review Ritual

Every morning, spend 5–10 minutes reviewing what happened overnight. Open TamoWork's dashboard on your computer, check the overnight reply log, and handle any escalated messages personally. This morning review is how you stay in control of your Instagram relationships even with full overnight automation running.

Overnight Reply Tips for Maximum Sales

  • Make it easy to place an order via DM. If your ordering process requires a website visit or a complicated back-and-forth, overnight automation cannot complete the sale. Simplify your order flow so a customer can confirm an order entirely through DMs.
  • Include payment links in your FAQ replies when appropriate. "You can pay via PayPal at [link]" closes the loop completely without you being there.
  • Review your overnight messages every morning without exception. This habit keeps you informed and catches any issues before they grow.
  • Let regulars know you have overnight coverage. A simple story or post saying "We now reply to DMs 24 hours a day — message us anytime!" sets customer expectations and encourages after-hours shopping.
