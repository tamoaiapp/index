Step-by-Step: Setting Up Automatic Comment Replies

Step 1

Install TamoWork on Your Computer

Download TamoWork from tamowork.com. Run the installer — it is a simple next-next-finish process. TamoWork includes the AI model built in. You do not need to sign up for any external AI service or obtain any API key. Everything runs on your machine.

Step 2

Connect Your Instagram Business Account

In TamoWork, go to Accounts → Instagram and click Connect Account. You will be redirected to Instagram's authorization page. Log in and grant TamoWork permission to manage comments on your behalf. This uses Instagram's official API — it is safe and compliant with their terms of service.

Step 3

Describe Your Business and Products

Go to AI Settings → Business Profile. Write a detailed description of your business. Include what products you sell, your prices or price ranges, sizes or variants available, shipping information, and ordering process. The more specific you are, the more accurate the comment replies will be.

Step 4

Build a Comment FAQ

Instagram comments tend to be short and repetitive. Add a set of one-line questions and answers specifically designed for comment-style inquiries. Examples: "Price?" → "Prices start at $20, DM us for the full catalog." "Shipping?" → "We ship nationwide, 3–5 business days." Short, helpful, and complete.

Step 5

Set Comment Reply Rules

In Instagram Settings → Comments, configure which types of comments to reply to automatically. You can set rules like: reply to all comments that contain a question mark, or reply to comments on specific types of posts (product posts only, not lifestyle posts). You can also set comments that contain negative keywords to be escalated to you for personal review.

Step 6

Set a Reply Delay

Configure how quickly replies are sent. A delay of 2–5 minutes makes replies feel more natural. Instant replies on every comment can sometimes look overly automated. A small delay keeps the human feel while still being far faster than manual replies.

Step 7

Activate and Monitor

Enable comment automation and let it run. Check the comment reply log at least once a day for the first week. Look for any replies that were off-target or could be improved, and refine your FAQ or business profile accordingly. After the initial tuning period, most business owners check the log once every few days.

Types of Comments You Should Always Handle Manually

Automation handles routine questions beautifully, but some comments need a human touch:

  • Complaints about orders or products
  • Comments from influential accounts or potential collaborators
  • Detailed custom order requests
  • Negative reviews or controversial statements

Configure TamoWork to escalate these to your notification list so you can reply personally.

The Engagement Snowball Effect

When you reply consistently and quickly to comments, something interesting happens: people comment more. They know they will get a response. Your posts generate more conversation. Instagram notices and shows your content to more people. More people comment, get replies, and the cycle continues. Fast, automated comment replies can be one of the highest-ROI actions you take for your Instagram growth.
