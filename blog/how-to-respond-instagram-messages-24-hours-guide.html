Your customers do not shop on a 9-to-5 schedule. They browse Instagram at night, during their lunch break, on weekends, and on holidays. When they find something they want, they message you immediately. And if you are asleep, busy, or simply have your phone off, that message sits unanswered until you get back to it.

For small businesses, this gap between when customers reach out and when they receive a response is where sales die. This guide shows you how to build a 24-hour Instagram response system using a free AI employee that never sleeps and never calls in sick.

The Economics of After-Hours Messages

Consider a typical small business Instagram account. If you look at when messages arrive, a significant portion — often 30–40% — come in between 8pm and 8am. These are the messages most likely to go unanswered overnight. They are also messages from people who have time to browse, time to consider, and time to buy. Capturing those after-hours inquiries can meaningfully increase your monthly revenue without changing anything else about your business.

Step-by-Step: Setting Up 24-Hour Instagram Responses

Step 1

Install TamoWork on Your Computer

Download and install TamoWork from tamowork.com. The process takes about 3 minutes. TamoWork runs as a background service on your computer — you do not need to keep it open in a window. As long as the computer is on and connected to the internet, TamoWork monitors your Instagram and responds automatically.

Step 2

Set Up Your Computer to Stay On Overnight

For true 24/7 coverage, your computer needs to be on. On Windows, go to Power Settings → Change plan settings → Never put the computer to sleep. On Mac, go to System Settings → Battery → Prevent automatic sleeping when the display is off. If keeping your main computer on is not practical, an old laptop you are not using also works perfectly for running TamoWork overnight.

Step 3

Connect Your Instagram Account

In TamoWork, go to Accounts → Instagram → Add Account. Complete the authorization. TamoWork will immediately start monitoring your inbox. For 24-hour coverage, make sure the "Always Active" option is enabled in TamoWork's settings so it never pauses.

Step 4

Create a Comprehensive Business Profile

At 2am, the AI handles inquiries completely on its own — there is no backup option to escalate to you in real time. This means your business profile needs to be thorough enough to handle any common question without gaps. Spend extra time on this step. Include everything a customer might ask: all product types, all price points, all delivery areas, all payment methods, your return window, and how to reach you for complex issues.

Step 5

Set Different Behavior for Business Hours vs. After Hours

TamoWork lets you configure different response behavior depending on the time of day. During business hours, you might want to handle some messages personally, so you can set TamoWork to only auto-respond after a 5-minute delay (giving you time to reply first). After hours, set it to instant auto-reply. This hybrid approach gives you the best of both worlds.

Step 6

Set Up a Morning Review Routine

Every morning, spend 5–10 minutes reviewing what happened overnight. Open TamoWork's dashboard and look at the conversation log. Did any customers follow up after the AI's reply? Did any messages need escalation? Were there any new questions your FAQ does not yet cover? Use this review to keep improving the system.

Step 7

Handle Escalated Messages Each Morning

TamoWork will flag messages that it could not handle confidently — complex requests, complaints, or unusual situations. Review these each morning and respond personally. Having a clear "handle by 10am" rule for escalated messages means nothing falls through the cracks, and your response time for complex issues is still under 12 hours even for overnight messages.

Tips for Maximizing After-Hours Sales

  • Make it easy to complete a purchase via DM. If your ordering process requires a back-and-forth that takes hours, after-hours automation cannot complete the sale. Simplify: clear prices, simple ordering steps, payment link in the DM.
  • Include a next step in every AI reply. "Would you like to place an order?" or "I can send you the full catalog — just reply yes!" gives the customer something to do even at midnight.
  • Review your most common after-hours questions. These reveal what information is missing from your profile or website — fix those gaps and watch your AI replies improve automatically.
