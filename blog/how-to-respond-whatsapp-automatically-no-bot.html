Step-by-Step Setup

Step 1

Install TamoWork on Your Computer

Download TamoWork from tamowork.com and run the installer. The installation takes about 3-5 minutes and includes everything you need — the AI model is bundled in, so there are no additional setup steps after installation. TamoWork sets itself to start automatically when your computer turns on.

Step 2

Connect WhatsApp Business via QR Code

In TamoWork, go to Accounts — WhatsApp — Connect and scan the displayed QR code using your WhatsApp Business app (Settings — Linked Devices — Link a Device). This is exactly like connecting WhatsApp Web. Once connected, TamoWork monitors all incoming messages in real time.

Step 3

Write Your Business Knowledge Base

Go to AI Settings — Business Profile. This is where the AI learns about your business. Write a thorough, plain-language description covering: what you sell, your prices, how customers buy from you, your location (if relevant), delivery options, payment methods, and your return policy. The more specific you are, the more accurate the automated replies will be.

Step 4

Add Your FAQ Entries

Go to AI Settings — FAQ. Add every question you get asked more than once, with your preferred answer. Do not try to make answers sound formal — write them the way you would actually say them in a WhatsApp message. Natural, conversational answers work best. Include common questions like: "Do you deliver?", "What's the price?", "How do I pay?", "When will my order arrive?"

Step 5

Configure Response Behavior

In WhatsApp Settings — Auto-Reply, turn on automatic responses. Set a reply delay of 1-3 minutes. Enable escalation alerts so you are notified on your desktop (not your phone — you are trying to reduce phone interruptions) when a message requires personal handling, such as a complaint or an unusual request.

Step 6

Test With Realistic Scenarios

Before going live, test TamoWork with a variety of messages. Ask questions the way your real customers ask them — often abbreviated, sometimes with typos, sometimes vague. Good test messages: "hi how much", "do u deliver to brooklyn", "whats ur return policy", "can i get a discount if i buy 10". Verify the AI handles these naturally and accurately.

Step 7

Go Live and Enter the Refinement Phase

Enable live automation. For the first two weeks, review the reply log every day. When you see a response that was off — wrong information, wrong tone, or missed the customer's intent — update your FAQ or business profile accordingly. After two weeks of active refinement, most users need very little ongoing attention to keep the system running well.

The Difference Between an AI Employee and a Bot

There is an important distinction worth making. A traditional WhatsApp "bot" uses keyword matching — if you type "price" it sends you a price list, if you type "delivery" it sends delivery info. It does not understand context or nuance.

TamoWork's AI employee works like a person. It reads the full message, understands the intent, checks your business information, and composes a relevant reply. A customer who asks "will my order arrive before Christmas if I order today?" gets a specific, contextual answer — not a generic delivery FAQ dump. That difference in quality is what keeps customers engaged and drives sales.
