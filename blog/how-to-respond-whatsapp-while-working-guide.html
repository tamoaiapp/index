Step 2

Turn Off WhatsApp Notifications on Your Phone

Go to your phone's notification settings and silence or disable WhatsApp Business notifications. This is the liberating step. You no longer need to be alerted every time a message arrives because TamoWork handles it. Set a specific time (like 10am and 4pm) when you will check TamoWork's dashboard for anything that needs personal attention. The rest of your day is yours to focus.

Step 3

Build a Business Profile That Works Without You

Since TamoWork will be handling messages while you are fully occupied with other work, your business profile needs to be comprehensive enough to operate independently. Write a thorough description of your business covering all products, prices, and processes. Include edge cases. Think about every question that has ever come in and make sure the answer is in your profile or FAQ.

Step 4

Categorize Your Messages by Response Type

Think about your typical WhatsApp messages and categorize them into three groups: (1) Routine — questions the AI can handle completely, (2) Semi-routine — questions where the AI can provide partial information and queue for your review, (3) Priority — messages requiring your immediate personal attention. Configure TamoWork's escalation settings to match this categorization.

Step 5

Set Up Batch Review Windows

Instead of reacting to WhatsApp in real time, schedule two or three fixed "review windows" each day — for example, 9am, 1pm, and 5pm. At each window, spend 10 minutes reviewing TamoWork's conversation log, responding personally to anything in your escalation queue, and updating your FAQ if you see any patterns that need addressing. Outside these windows, you are focused entirely on your real work.

Step 6

Configure Priority Alerts for Truly Urgent Messages

Even during deep-work sessions, some messages cannot wait. Configure TamoWork to send you a specific notification (a desktop alert or a different sound) for messages that match urgent criteria. This way, you are never fully disconnected from genuinely time-sensitive situations, but you are also not interrupted by routine messages every few minutes.

Step 7

Review and Refine During Your Scheduled Windows

Use your daily review windows to improve TamoWork's performance. Any message in the last session that was handled imperfectly becomes a new FAQ entry. Any new question type you see for the first time gets added to the knowledge base. These regular improvements compound over time — the system gets better every week with minimal effort.
