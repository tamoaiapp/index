Step-by-Step: AI-Powered WhatsApp Sales

Step 1

Install TamoWork and Connect WhatsApp Business

Download TamoWork from tamowork.com and install it. Connect your WhatsApp Business account via the QR code in TamoWork's account settings. From this point forward, every incoming WhatsApp message is monitored and can be automatically replied to with AI-generated responses based on your business information.

Step 2

Write a Sales-Focused Business Profile

In AI Settings — Business Profile, write your business description with sales conversion in mind. Include your value proposition, what makes your product or service the best choice, and concrete proof points (years in business, number of happy customers, specific certifications or awards). Give the AI real sales language to work with, not just factual information.

Step 3

Build a Conversion-Optimized FAQ

In your FAQ, each answer should not just inform — it should sell. For example: "How much does it cost?" should not just list a price. It should list the price AND explain what is included AND ideally include a social proof statement: "Our most popular package is $85 and includes delivery. Most customers order by Wednesday for weekend delivery." Each answer closes the information gap AND moves toward a purchase.

Step 4

Add Clear Ordering Instructions to Every Relevant Reply

Configure TamoWork so that every FAQ reply related to products or pricing includes a clear, simple call to action. Do not assume customers know how to order — tell them explicitly. "To place your order, just reply with [item name], [quantity], and your delivery address. We'll confirm and send payment details right away." Make it frictionless.

Step 5

Configure Zero-Delay Responses for Inquiries

Set TamoWork's response delay to 1 minute or less for all messages. In WhatsApp sales, the first business to respond almost always wins. When a customer is comparing options and you reply in 60 seconds while competitors reply in 2 hours, the sale is almost certainly yours. Speed is your competitive advantage — and with TamoWork, it is automatic.

Step 6

Set Up Upsell Suggestions in Your Responses

Train TamoWork to suggest complementary products or upgrades where appropriate. If someone asks about your basic package, the AI can mention: "Many customers also add [complementary service] for [price] — it's a great combination." Include these upsell suggestions in relevant FAQ entries. Even if 1 in 10 customers takes the upsell, it meaningfully increases your average order value.

Step 7

Enable Follow-Up for Inactive Conversations

Some customers ask about your products, get all the information they need, and then go quiet. They are interested but undecided. Configure TamoWork's follow-up feature to send a gentle message after 24-48 hours of inactivity: "Hi! Just checking in to see if you have any more questions or if you'd like to go ahead with your order. Happy to help!" This recaptures a significant number of warm leads that would otherwise be lost.

Tracking Your WhatsApp Sales Performance

TamoWork's reporting dashboard shows you key metrics that directly relate to sales performance:

  • Messages received vs. replied — your response rate (should be 100%)
  • Average response time — should be under 2 minutes
  • Conversation outcomes — how many conversations ended in an order
  • After-hours volume — how many inquiries came in outside your working hours

Use these metrics weekly to identify where improvements in your FAQ or business profile would most improve your conversion rate. The data tells you exactly where the opportunities are.
