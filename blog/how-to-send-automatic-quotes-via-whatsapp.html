How to Send Automatic Quotes via WhatsApp

🗓 March 22, 2026⏱ 8 min read

For many small businesses — custom cakes, renovation services, photography, catering, personalized gifts — quoting is the business. A customer messages you asking "how much for X?", you figure out the price, and then you reply. This exchange happens dozens of times a day and eats up massive amounts of time.

The good news: a large portion of your quoting process is predictable and can be automated. If you know your pricing structure, your AI employee can provide accurate quotes 24/7 without you being involved — freeing you to focus on the actual work instead of the inbox.

What Makes Quoting on WhatsApp So Time-Consuming

The challenge with quote requests is that they feel personal and require information about the customer's specific needs. But most quote requests actually follow the same patterns. For a custom cake business, 80% of quote requests ask about: number of servings, flavor preferences, complexity of decoration, and delivery date. If you can teach your AI employee these pricing factors and your pricing formula, it can handle the initial quote in seconds.

This does not mean full automation of every quote — some complex requests genuinely need your judgment. But handling the initial information gathering and preliminary quote automatically saves enormous time and keeps customers engaged while they wait.

Step-by-Step: Automating WhatsApp Quotes

Step 1

Document Your Pricing Structure

Before touching TamoWork, write down your complete pricing in clear, structured terms. For service businesses: base rates, add-on pricing, minimum order amounts, bulk discounts. For product businesses: all SKUs with prices, volume tiers, bundle options. Make it comprehensive — this becomes the AI's pricing reference. If your pricing has been "in your head," now is the time to write it all down.

Step 2

Install TamoWork and Connect WhatsApp

Download TamoWork from tamowork.com and install it. Connect your WhatsApp Business account via the QR code link (Accounts — WhatsApp — Connect in TamoWork, then scan with WhatsApp Business — Settings — Linked Devices). TamoWork will immediately start receiving your incoming messages.

Step 3

Enter Your Pricing in TamoWork's Business Profile

Go to AI Settings — Business Profile and include your full pricing structure. Be explicit about what is included at each price point and what triggers a higher price. Examples: "Custom cakes: $80 for up to 20 servings with standard decoration, $120 for up to 30 servings, $50 additional for tiered designs, $30 additional for sugar flowers." The more granular, the better the AI's quote accuracy.

Step 4

Create Quote-Specific FAQ Entries

Add FAQ entries that specifically address the quoting process. Include: what information you need to provide a quote, how to interpret ambiguous quote requests, and what a typical quote conversation looks like. You can also include example quotes — "A customer asking for a birthday cake for 25 people with custom writing would typically be $95 including delivery in our service area."

Step 5

Set Up Quote Request Follow-Up Questions

Configure TamoWork to ask clarifying questions when a quote request is incomplete. For example, if someone messages "how much for a cake?" the AI should respond with the starting price and ask what they need: "Our cakes start at $75. To give you an exact price, could you tell me: how many servings, what occasion, and do you have a specific design in mind?" This moves the conversation forward while gathering the information needed for an accurate quote.

Step 6

Define Quote Escalation Thresholds

Set a threshold above which quote requests should be escalated to you personally. For example, any order over $500 might warrant a personal touch. Configure TamoWork to handle these by collecting initial information, providing a price range, and then flagging the conversation for your personal follow-up: "This project sounds like it falls in the $400-600 range. Let me have our team review the details and get back to you with a precise quote within [X hours]."

Step 7

Test with Your Most Common Quote Types

Run TamoWork in Test Mode and simulate your five most common quote requests. For each, verify that the AI: understands what is being asked, provides accurate pricing information, asks relevant follow-up questions if needed, and gives clear next steps for the customer. Refine your pricing documentation and FAQ until test results are consistently accurate.
