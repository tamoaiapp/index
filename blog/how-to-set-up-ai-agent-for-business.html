How to Set Up an AI Agent for Your Business in 30 Minutes

🗓 March 23, 2026⏱ 8 min read

Getting an AI agent working for your business doesn't require technical expertise, a large budget, or days of configuration. If you can fill out a form and describe your business in plain language, you can have an AI agent running in 30 minutes. Here's the complete process.

Before You Start: What You'll Need

Gather these items before you begin:

  • A Windows computer with at least 8GB of RAM
  • A stable internet connection (for the initial download)
  • Your Instagram business account login
  • Your WhatsApp number (or WhatsApp Business)
  • Basic information about your business (we'll detail what this means below)

That's it. No API key, no OpenAI account, no coding experience needed.

Step 1: Download and Install TamoWork (5 minutes)

Go to tamowork.com and download the installer. It's a standard Windows application — double-click, follow the prompts, and it installs like any other software. The first time you launch it, it will download the AI model files. Depending on your internet speed, this takes 10–20 minutes. You can leave it running in the background while you prepare the next step.

Step 2: Prepare Your Business Information (10 minutes)

This is the most important step and requires no technical knowledge — just knowledge of your own business. Write down (in plain language) answers to these questions:

  • What does your business do / what do you sell?
  • What are your products or services, with prices?
  • What are your operating hours?
  • Where are you located? Do you deliver?
  • What payment methods do you accept?
  • What's your return or cancellation policy?
  • What are the most common questions your customers ask?

You don't need to write essays — concise, clear information is better than lengthy explanations. The AI agent will use this information to answer customer questions.

Step 3: Enter Your Business Information (5 minutes)

Open TamoWork and navigate to the Business Profile section. Enter the information you gathered in Step 2. The interface is simple — text fields and dropdowns, nothing complex. This information becomes your AI agent's knowledge base — the foundation for all its responses.

Step 4: Connect Instagram (3 minutes)

In TamoWork's Instagram section, you'll scan a QR code with your Instagram app or log in with your credentials to connect your account. Once connected, you can toggle on automatic replies for DMs and/or comments. You can set different responses for each type of interaction.

Step 5: Connect WhatsApp (3 minutes)

Similar to Instagram — scan a QR code with your WhatsApp app to connect. Your WhatsApp messages will now be monitored and responded to by the AI agent. You can still send manual messages; the AI handles the incoming ones you haven't responded to.

Step 6: Test Your Setup (5 minutes)

From a friend's phone or a test account, send a few messages to your Instagram and WhatsApp — the kinds of questions your real customers typically ask. Check that the responses are accurate and appropriate. If anything needs adjustment, update your business information and test again.

Common First-Day Adjustments

After your first day with the AI agent live, review the conversations it had:

  • Were there questions it couldn't answer? Add that information to your knowledge base.
  • Were any responses inaccurate? Correct the relevant information.
  • Was the tone appropriate for your brand? Adjust the style settings if needed.

Most businesses find their AI agent working well from day one. A day or two of minor adjustments based on real conversations usually gets it to an excellent standard.

The Result

After 30 minutes of setup, you have an AI employee that works 24/7 on your Instagram and WhatsApp. It answers questions, handles inquiries, and keeps customers engaged — at zero ongoing cost. The time you invested in setup pays dividends every single day.
