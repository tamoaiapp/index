On the TamoWork dashboard, click on "Instagram Employee." This is the AI that will handle your DMs and comments. You'll be asked to connect your Instagram account — this is done securely through the official Instagram login window.

Train Your AI Employee

This is the most important step. You'll fill out a simple form telling your AI employee about your business: what you sell, your prices, your location, your opening hours, and how you like to talk to customers. The more detail you give, the better the replies will be.

Set Your Reply Preferences

Choose whether you want the AI to reply to DMs, to comments, or to both. You can also set rules like "always ask for the customer's phone number" or "always mention our current promotion." These rules are applied to every single reply automatically.

Step 6

Run a Test

Before going live, send yourself a test DM from a secondary Instagram account. Watch how TamoWork responds. If the tone or information isn't right, go back and adjust your training information. Most users get it right on the second or third try.

Step 7

Activate and Leave It Running

Once you're happy with the test, click "Activate" and leave TamoWork running in the background on your PC. It will now reply to Instagram messages automatically while you focus on the rest of your business — or simply sleep.
