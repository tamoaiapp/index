How to Set Up an AI Employee for WhatsApp: Step-by-Step

March 22, 2026  ·  6 min read

Every business that uses WhatsApp to communicate with customers faces the same challenge: the messages never stop, and each one demands a timely response. You can't ignore customers, but you also can't spend your entire day glued to WhatsApp. There has to be a better way to manage this.

An AI employee for WhatsApp is the answer — and setting one up is simpler than you might think. This step-by-step tutorial walks you through the entire process of installing and configuring a free AI employee that handles WhatsApp customer messages around the clock on your own computer.

Why You Need This

WhatsApp automation typically costs money — either through the official Business API with per-message pricing, or through third-party automation tools with monthly subscriptions. For small businesses operating on tight margins, these costs add up and often don't deliver enough value to justify the expense.

TamoWork changes this equation. It's a free Windows application that connects to your WhatsApp via the Linked Devices feature (no API required) and uses a local AI model to read and reply to messages. The AI learns your business from information you provide, and then represents your business professionally to every customer — for free, forever.

Step by Step: How to Do It

Step 1

Prepare Your WhatsApp Account

Make sure you have WhatsApp installed on your smartphone and that your WhatsApp account is active. If you're using WhatsApp Business (the business version of the app), it works just as well. Have your phone charged and connected to the internet before starting the TamoWork setup.

Step 2

Download and Install TamoWork

Visit tamowork.com on your Windows PC. Click "Download free." Run the installer file and follow the installation prompts. The process takes about 3 minutes. No registration or email required.

Step 3

Open TamoWork and Select WhatsApp

Launch TamoWork from the desktop shortcut. On the main dashboard, click "WhatsApp Employee." The WhatsApp connection screen will appear, showing a QR code. Keep this screen open and pick up your phone.

Step 4

Scan the QR Code to Connect

On your phone, open WhatsApp and go to Settings (the three dots menu on Android, or the Settings tab on iPhone). Tap "Linked Devices" and then "Link a Device." Point your phone's camera at the QR code on your computer screen. The connection happens instantly.

Step 5

Train Your AI Employee

With WhatsApp connected, you'll see the training panel. Fill it in carefully: describe your business, list your products and prices, explain your delivery options and timelines, share your location and business hours, and write out answers to common customer questions. The richer your training data, the better your AI employee performs.

Step 6

Configure Automation Settings

Decide how your AI employee should operate: reply to all messages or only messages from new contacts; use instant replies or add a short delay to seem more natural; set up a welcome message for first-time contacts; and configure which types of messages should be forwarded to you for personal handling.

Step 7

Test and Activate

Send a test WhatsApp message from a secondary number (or have a friend send one). Evaluate the AI's reply. If you're happy with it, click "Activate." Minimize TamoWork to the system tray. Your WhatsApp AI employee is now live and working around the clock.

Important Tips

  • Linked Devices is reliable: WhatsApp's Linked Devices feature (which TamoWork uses) is the same technology used by WhatsApp Web. It's stable and works without your phone needing to be constantly active.
  • Good training = good results: Spend extra time on your training data. Business owners who write detailed, complete training information see dramatically better results than those who rush through it.
  • Update for promotions: Whenever you have a special offer, new product, or price change, update your AI employee's training data so it can mention it in conversations.
  • Zero per-message cost: Unlike API-based solutions, TamoWork doesn't charge per message. You can receive and reply to thousands of messages per month with no additional cost.
