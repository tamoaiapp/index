How to Set Up AI for Instagram — Complete Guide

Step 1

Convert to an Instagram Business Account

If you are still using a personal Instagram account, you need to switch to a Business or Creator account first. Go to your Instagram profile → Settings → Account → Switch to Professional Account. Choose Business, select your category, and complete the setup. This is free and takes about 3 minutes.

Step 2

Download and Install TamoWork

Go to tamowork.com and download the installer for your system. Run it and follow the simple setup wizard. TamoWork installs the AI model on your machine automatically — you do not need to configure anything technical. The whole installation process takes under 5 minutes.

Step 3

Authorize Your Instagram Account in TamoWork

Open TamoWork and go to Accounts → Instagram → Add Account. Click Connect and complete the authorization. TamoWork will ask for permission to read and reply to your DMs and comments. Grant these permissions. Your login is handled by Instagram's secure system — TamoWork never sees your password.

Step 4

Build Your Business Knowledge Base

Navigate to AI Settings → Business Profile. Fill in every field you can. Include: your business name and type, what you sell, your price ranges, how customers place orders, payment methods you accept, delivery areas and timeframes, return policy, and your working hours. The AI learns from this to give accurate answers.

Step 5

Add Frequent Questions and Preferred Answers

Go to AI Settings → FAQ. Add each common question with your exact preferred answer. If you want the AI to always mention your WhatsApp number when someone asks about custom orders, write that into the FAQ answer. You have full control over the responses.

Step 6

Configure What to Automate

In Instagram Settings, choose which actions to automate: DM replies, comment replies, or both. You can also set rules — for example, only auto-reply to DMs from accounts that follow you, or always escalate messages containing the word "complaint" to your notification queue instead of auto-replying.

Step 7

Run a Test Before Going Live

Before enabling full automation, use the Test Mode feature. In test mode, TamoWork generates replies but does not send them — it just shows you what it would have said. Send a few test messages from a friend's account and review the drafted replies. Adjust your business profile or FAQ until you are happy with the quality.

Step 8

Enable Live Automation

When you are satisfied with the test results, switch TamoWork from Test Mode to Live Mode. Your AI employee is now active. It will monitor your Instagram account continuously and reply to new messages as they arrive.

What to Do After Setup

The first week is about refinement. Check the reply log every day, look for any responses that felt off, and improve your business profile based on what you see. Most users get to a point where 95%+ of replies are excellent within the first 5–7 days.

After that, the maintenance is minimal — mainly updating your FAQ when you launch new products or change your policies.

Common Setup Mistakes to Avoid

  • Vague business descriptions. "I sell clothes" gives the AI nothing to work with. "I sell women's casual wear sizes XS–XXL, prices from $25–$95, shipping 3–5 days nationwide" gives it everything.
  • Skipping the test phase. Always test before going live. A few minutes of testing prevents embarrassing replies.
  • Not updating after product changes. If you add new products or change your prices, update TamoWork's business profile the same day.
