Why Most "AI for WhatsApp" Solutions Are Too Expensive

The official WhatsApp Business API costs money per message. On top of that, most services that wrap around the API charge a monthly subscription. For a small business owner sending and receiving hundreds of messages per day, this adds up quickly. The tools designed for enterprise customers simply do not make financial sense for a local bakery, clothing store, or service provider with tight margins.

TamoWork takes a fundamentally different approach: the AI model runs locally on your computer. No cloud processing, no per-message fees, no subscription. You pay exactly nothing after the initial download.

What You Need

  • WhatsApp Business app installed on your phone
  • A computer with Windows, Mac, or Linux and 4GB+ RAM
  • TamoWork (free at tamowork.com)

Step-by-Step: Setting Up AI for WhatsApp Business

Step 1

Ensure You Have WhatsApp Business (Not Regular WhatsApp)

TamoWork works with WhatsApp Business. If you are currently using the regular WhatsApp app, download WhatsApp Business from your app store and migrate your number. This is free. Your contacts, chat history, and profile all transfer automatically. WhatsApp Business also gives you additional features like business hours, catalog, and auto-reply templates.

Step 2

Download and Install TamoWork

Go to tamowork.com on your computer. Download the installer for your operating system and run it. The installation takes about 3-5 minutes. TamoWork bundles the AI model with the installer, so there are no additional downloads needed after installation is complete.

Step 3

Link Your WhatsApp Business Account

Open TamoWork. In the left sidebar, click Accounts — Add Account — WhatsApp Business. TamoWork shows a QR code. On your phone, open WhatsApp Business, go to the three-dot menu — Linked Devices — Link a Device — and scan the QR code. Your WhatsApp is now connected. TamoWork will immediately begin showing your recent conversations in the dashboard.

Step 4

Configure Your AI Employee's Knowledge Base

This is the most important step. Go to AI Settings — Business Profile. Write everything your AI employee needs to know to serve your customers: your business description, all products and services with prices, how customers can order, what payment methods you accept, your delivery options, and your policies. The AI uses this as its primary reference for generating replies.

Step 5

Create a Comprehensive FAQ

Go to AI Settings — FAQ. This is where you store specific question-and-answer pairs. The FAQ complements your business profile — where the profile handles general context, the FAQ handles specific, common questions. Add your 20-30 most frequently asked questions. Update this regularly as you notice new patterns in your customer inquiries.

Step 6

Define Your AI Employee's Tone and Style

Go to AI Settings — Communication Style. Choose whether your business communicates formally or casually, with emojis or without, in full sentences or brief bullet points. You can also add example messages in your preferred style for the AI to learn from. Getting the tone right makes replies feel authentic to your brand.

Step 7

Enable Automation and Configure Escalation

In WhatsApp Settings — Automation, toggle on Auto-Reply. Set your preferred response delay. Then configure escalation triggers — conditions under which the AI should not reply automatically but instead add the message to your personal review queue. Recommended escalation triggers: messages containing "complaint," "refund," "cancel," or messages from contacts flagged as VIP.

Step 8

Run a Test Session and Go Live

Enable Test Mode in TamoWork and send yourself several test messages covering different scenarios: a simple price question, a custom order request, an inquiry about delivery, and a complaint. Review each generated reply. When you are satisfied with the quality across all scenarios, switch from Test Mode to Live Mode. Your AI employee is now officially on the job.

What to Expect in the First Week

The first week is a refinement period. Check TamoWork's reply log every day. Note any replies that missed the mark — then add the relevant question to your FAQ with a better answer. By day five or six, most users find their AI employee is handling 90%+ of messages well, with minimal personal intervention required. After two weeks, the system becomes largely self-sufficient.

Advanced Configuration Options

  • Working hours mode — set TamoWork to instantly auto-reply outside your working hours but queue messages during business hours for your review first
  • Language detection — if you serve customers in multiple languages, TamoWork can detect the language of incoming messages and reply in kind
  • Contact labels — tag VIP customers or known problem accounts for special handling rules
