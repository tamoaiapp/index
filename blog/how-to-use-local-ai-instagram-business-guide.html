Step by Step: How to Do It

Step 1

Check Your Computer Requirements

TamoWork requires Windows 10 or 11 and at least 4GB of RAM (8GB recommended for best performance). Most modern PCs easily meet this requirement. You also need a stable internet connection for Instagram communication.

Step 2

Download and Install TamoWork

Visit tamowork.com and click the download button. The installer is about 200MB and includes the local AI model. Installation takes about 3 minutes. No OpenAI account needed.

Step 3

Let the Local AI Model Load

On first launch, TamoWork will initialize the local AI model. This may take 1–2 minutes the first time. Once loaded, it runs instantly on subsequent launches. The AI model lives on your hard drive — never in the cloud.

Step 4

Connect Instagram and Train Your AI Employee

Log into Instagram through TamoWork's secure login screen. Then fill out the business training form: describe what you sell, your prices, delivery options, return policies, and your preferred communication style. This trains the local AI to speak on your behalf.

Step 5

Choose What to Automate

Decide whether to automate DMs, comment replies, or both. Each can be configured independently. For example, you might want to auto-reply to all DMs but only auto-reply to comments that contain certain keywords like "price" or "buy."

Step 6

Run Tests and Refine

Send test messages from a secondary account. Review the replies. Adjust your training information if anything sounds off or inaccurate. The more specific your training data, the more accurate and helpful the AI responses will be.

Step 7

Activate and Let It Work

Enable the Instagram Employee and let TamoWork run in your system tray. Your local AI is now live — handling Instagram customer interactions 24/7, powered entirely by your own computer.
