Product Photo Requirements for Amazon, Shopify and Etsy

TamoWork · 2026-04-13

Each platform has different requirements for product photos. Using the wrong size or style directly affects your ranking and conversion. Here are the exact specs for every major platform.

Amazon: strict white background rules

Main image: pure white background (#FFFFFF), product fills 85%+ of frame, no watermarks, no additional text or graphics. Minimum 1000px on longest side (for zoom). Recommended: 2000x2000px JPEG.

Shopify: square for consistency

Square images (1:1) at 2048x2048px for best quality. JPEG for solid products, PNG for transparent backgrounds. Consistent style across all products creates a professional storefront feel.

Etsy: lifestyle images convert better

Etsy buyers respond to lifestyle and context photos. Main image: product clearly visible, square format. Include at least one lifestyle photo showing the product in use or in a real environment.

eBay: quantity wins

eBay allows up to 24 photos per listing. Listings with 10+ photos have significantly higher sold rates. Include every angle, detail shots, and condition documentation for used items.
