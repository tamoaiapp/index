Step by Step: AI Answering Messages for Your Instagram Business

🗓 March 22, 2026⏱ 8 min read

This is the complete guide — no assumptions, no skipped steps. If you have an Instagram business account and you want AI to start answering your messages automatically, by the end of this article you will have it fully set up and running. The whole process takes about 30 minutes, and the software is completely free.

We are going to use TamoWork, an AI employee tool that runs entirely on your own computer. No subscription, no OpenAI API, no cloud service. Your customer conversations stay private on your machine, and the AI runs with no ongoing cost.

Before You Start: What to Prepare

Gather these things before opening TamoWork for the first time — having them ready makes setup much faster:

  • Your Instagram Business account login credentials
  • A written description of your business and what you sell
  • A list of your products with prices
  • Your most frequently asked questions and preferred answers
  • Your shipping and delivery information
  • Your return or refund policy

If any of these are not written down yet, take 15 minutes to write them before starting. The quality of your AI employee is directly proportional to the quality of the information you give it.

System Requirements

  • Operating system: Windows 10 or 11, macOS 12+, or Ubuntu 20.04+
  • RAM: 4GB minimum (8GB recommended for best performance)
  • Storage: 5GB free space for the AI model
  • Internet connection (for Instagram API sync)

Complete Step-by-Step Setup

Step 1

Download TamoWork

Go to tamowork.com on your computer's browser. Click the Download button and select your operating system. Save the installer to your desktop or Downloads folder. The file size is around 400MB because it includes the local AI model.

Step 2

Install TamoWork

Run the downloaded installer file. On Windows: double-click the .exe and follow the wizard. On Mac: open the .dmg and drag TamoWork to your Applications folder. On Linux: run the provided installation script. The first launch takes a minute or two as the AI model initializes. This is normal.

Step 3

Connect Your Instagram Business Account

In TamoWork's sidebar, click Accounts — Add Account — Instagram. A browser window will open asking you to log in to Instagram and grant TamoWork permission to manage messages and comments. Log in, grant the permissions, and return to TamoWork. You should see your account name appear with a green "Connected" status.

Step 4

Enter Your Business Information

Click AI Settings — Business Profile. In the large text field, paste or type your business description. Use natural language. Include: business name, what you sell, your typical customer, your location or service area, your unique selling points, and any important policies customers should know.

Step 5

Add Your Products and Pricing

Still in AI Settings, go to Products. Add each product with its name, description, price, and any variants. If you have many products, group them by category. This data allows the AI to answer pricing and availability questions accurately.

Step 6

Build Your FAQ Database

Go to AI Settings — FAQ. Add at minimum 15 question-and-answer pairs covering your most common inquiries. Format each entry with the exact question as a customer might phrase it, followed by your ideal complete answer. Good FAQ entries eliminate follow-up questions and move customers toward purchasing.

Step 7

Set Your Communication Style

In AI Settings — Style, choose your preferred communication tone. Write two or three example sentences in the exact style you want — these are style samples the AI uses as a reference. Also add any phrases you want to avoid and any phrases you always use.

Step 8

Configure Automation Rules

Go to Instagram Settings — Automation. Enable DM Auto-Reply and/or Comment Auto-Reply. Set your response delay (1-3 minutes is recommended). Configure escalation rules — which types of messages should be flagged for your personal review instead of handled automatically.

Step 9

Test Before Going Live

Use TamoWork's Test Mode to simulate incoming messages. Send yourself test DMs from another account and watch TamoWork generate (but not send) responses. Review each one carefully. Is the information accurate? Is the tone right? Adjust your settings until you are fully satisfied. Only then switch to Live Mode.

Step 10

Monitor and Refine for the First Week

After going live, check the reply log every day for the first week. Note any answers that were off and update your FAQ or business profile to address them. Most users find that after 5-7 days of refinement, their TamoWork setup runs with minimal intervention needed. By week two, you are genuinely hands-off for routine messages.

Ongoing Maintenance

After the initial refinement period, TamoWork needs very little maintenance:

  • Update product info when you add items or change prices
  • Review the reply log once or twice a week
  • Add new FAQ entries when you notice repeated questions
  • Check that TamoWork is running each morning

That is it. Your AI employee handles the rest — 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, at zero ongoing cost.
