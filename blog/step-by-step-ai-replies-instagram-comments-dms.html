March 22, 2026  ·  7 min read

On Instagram, customer contact comes from two places: Direct Messages (DMs) and comments on your posts. Most automation tools handle one or the other — rarely both, and almost never for free. The result is that business owners end up automating DMs but still manually replying to comments, or vice versa, leaving half the work on their plate.

This tutorial covers both. You'll learn how to set up AI replies for Instagram comments and DMs simultaneously, using one free tool that runs entirely on your computer. No switching between apps. No subscription fees. Complete coverage.

Why You Need This

Comments and DMs serve different purposes in the customer journey. Comments are often public — a great reply there gets seen by everyone who reads the post. DMs are private and usually indicate higher purchase intent. Both need to be handled quickly and professionally to maximize conversions and customer satisfaction.

Switch to the Comments tab. Decide whether to reply to all comments or only those containing questions or specific keywords. Test DMs by sending from a secondary account. Test comments by posting a comment on one of your photos from the same secondary account. Verify both work correctly, then activate the Instagram Employee. Both DMs and comments are now handled automatically.

Important Tips

  • Comment replies are public: Remember that your comment replies are visible to everyone who reads the post. Make sure replies are professional and representative of your brand, since they act as marketing too.
  • DM replies are private: You have more flexibility in DMs to ask for phone numbers, addresses, or other details needed to complete a sale. Configure this in the DM settings.
  • Negative comments need special handling: Configure TamoWork to notify you of negative comments instead of auto-replying. Personal responses to complaints build more trust than automated ones.
  • Both run in parallel: TamoWork handles DMs and comments simultaneously without any performance issues. You don't need to choose one or the other.
