Step by Step: How to Do It

Step 1

Define What You Want the AI to Handle

Before installing anything, decide what types of WhatsApp messages you want the AI to handle. Good candidates: product inquiries, pricing questions, delivery questions, availability checks, store hours, location information. Messages that should still come to you: complaints, payment disputes, personalized order customizations, VIP customer relationships. This clarity will make your setup faster and more effective.

Step 2

Download and Install TamoWork for Free

Visit tamowork.com and download the Windows installer. Run it — it's a standard Windows application installation, no different from installing any other software. Takes about 3 minutes. No registration, no payment details, no trial period.

Step 3

Launch and Connect WhatsApp

Open TamoWork. Click "WhatsApp Employee." Wait for the QR code to appear. Open WhatsApp on your smartphone, go to Settings, then Linked Devices, then Link a Device. Scan the QR code. In about 10 seconds, your WhatsApp is connected to TamoWork.

Step 4

Build Your AI Employee's Knowledge Base

Write your business information in the TamoWork training form. Be thorough: list every product with its price and description, explain all your service options, describe your delivery methods and costs, state your payment options, give your business hours and location, and write clear answers to your top customer questions. Aim for comprehensive coverage — every detail you include makes your AI employee smarter.

Step 5

Define Your Business Voice and Rules

Configure how your AI employee communicates: how formal or casual, whether to use customer names, how to greet new contacts, how to handle repeat customers differently. Also set up your escalation rules from Step 1 — define exactly which types of messages should be flagged for your personal attention rather than auto-replied to.

Step 6

Run a Complete Test Session

Using a secondary phone number (or asking a friend to help), run through a realistic customer interaction sequence. Test: asking about your most popular product, asking for the price of something, asking about delivery to a specific area, asking a question the AI might not know, and sending a complaint. Review every reply and update your training data accordingly.

Step 7

Activate, Monitor, and Continuously Improve

Enable the WhatsApp Employee in TamoWork. For the first two weeks, check in daily — this is your tuning phase. Read conversations, identify gaps, and improve your training data. After this initial period, weekly check-ins are sufficient for most businesses. Your AI employee gets better over time as you refine its knowledge base based on real customer interactions.
