Step by Step: Automate WhatsApp for Your Small Business

March 22, 2026  ·  7 min read

WhatsApp is where customers come to buy. They've seen your product on Instagram or heard about your business through a friend, and now they're sending you a WhatsApp message to learn more or place an order. What happens next determines whether you make the sale or lose it.

For small businesses, the WhatsApp inbox can quickly become overwhelming. Orders, inquiries, complaints, follow-ups — all arriving in a single chat app that demands constant attention. This tutorial shows you how to automate the routine parts of your WhatsApp business communication, for free, using a local AI employee on your PC.

Why You Need This

Repetitive WhatsApp replies cost small business owners 1–3 hours every day. The same questions — prices, availability, delivery times — get answered manually over and over. That time could go toward creating more products, serving in-person customers, or simply resting so you have more energy to grow your business.

TamoWork automates this repetitive layer of WhatsApp communication. You train your AI employee once with your business information, and it handles the routine questions on autopilot. You stay in control of everything that genuinely needs your personal attention.

Step by Step: How to Do It

Step 1

Write Down Your Top 15 Customer Questions

Before touching any software, spend 5 minutes listing the questions you get most often on WhatsApp. These are your training priorities. Examples: "How much is X?", "Do you deliver to Y city?", "What payment methods do you accept?", "How long does shipping take?", "Can I order for Sunday?" This preparation makes setup much faster.

Step 2

Download and Install TamoWork

Go to tamowork.com and click "Download free." Install the application on your Windows PC. The installer is straightforward — just follow the prompts. Ready in about 3 minutes.

Step 3

Open TamoWork and Connect WhatsApp

Launch TamoWork. Click "WhatsApp Employee." When the QR code appears, open WhatsApp on your phone, go to Settings > Linked Devices > Link a Device, and scan the code. WhatsApp is now linked to your TamoWork AI employee.

Step 4

Enter Your Business Training Data

Use your list from Step 1. For each question, write the complete, accurate answer. Then add additional business context: your full product catalog with prices, your delivery options, your business hours, your location, and your payment methods. This becomes the AI's knowledge base.

Step 5

Configure How the AI Should Reply

Decide on the communication style: formal or conversational? Does it greet customers by name? Does it always end with a call to action? Does it ask qualifying questions to understand the customer's needs better before responding? These settings make your AI employee sound authentically like your business.

Step 6

Set Automation Boundaries

Choose what the AI handles and what it escalates to you. A good starting point: automate all routine product and service questions, but flag any message containing words like "problem," "wrong," "refund," or "urgent" for your personal attention. This keeps automation efficient while preserving human handling for sensitive situations.

Step 7

Run Tests, Activate, and Iterate

Send several test WhatsApp messages covering your most common scenarios. Review each reply carefully. Make any needed adjustments to your training data. Then activate the WhatsApp Employee. Check conversations daily for the first two weeks, improving your training data as you find gaps.

Important Tips

  • Start with the questions you hate answering most: If there's a particular question you answer 20 times a day, make sure your AI employee handles it perfectly. This alone can save you an hour per day.
  • Be specific, not generic: "We deliver to most areas" is weak training data. "We deliver within a 30km radius of downtown São Paulo, orders over R$100 qualify for free delivery" is strong training data.
  • Update during busy periods: Before Black Friday, Christmas, or any big sale, update your AI employee with the latest promotions and policies so it can handle the increased volume accurately.
  • Zero cost at any scale: Whether you get 50 WhatsApp messages per month or 5,000, TamoWork handles them all at the same cost: free.
