Step by Step: How to Do It

Step 1

Open your web browser and go to tamowork.com. Click the "Download free" button. The installer file (about 200MB) will download to your PC. No email signup required. No credit card needed. Just click and download.

Step 2

Run the Installer

Find the downloaded file in your Downloads folder and double-click it. Follow the installation wizard. When Windows asks if you want to allow the app to make changes, click "Yes." Choose your installation folder (the default is fine) and click "Install." Done in about 2 minutes.

Step 3

Launch TamoWork for the First Time

Double-click the TamoWork shortcut on your desktop. The first launch initializes the local AI model — this takes 1–2 minutes. You'll see a progress bar. Once complete, TamoWork opens to its main dashboard showing all available employees.

Step 4

Click "WhatsApp Employee"

From the dashboard, click "WhatsApp Employee." A setup panel opens with three sections: Connect Account, Business Training, and Automation Settings. You'll work through each of these in order.

Step 5

Connect Your WhatsApp Account

Click "Connect WhatsApp." A QR code appears on screen. Pick up your phone, open WhatsApp, tap the three-dot menu (Android) or Settings (iPhone), tap "Linked Devices," then "Link a Device," and finally point your camera at the QR code on your PC screen. Connection happens in seconds.

Step 6

Fill Out the Business Training Form

This is where you teach your AI employee about your business. Write detailed entries for: your business name and what you do, your products/services with prices, your location and business hours, your delivery and payment options, and at least 10 common customer questions with their answers. More detail equals better replies.

Step 7

Configure, Test, and Activate

In the Automation Settings section, choose your reply speed and escalation rules. Then send yourself a test WhatsApp from another number. Review the reply. If it's good, click "Activate." If it needs work, update your training data and test again. Once activated, your WhatsApp AI employee is live and ready to work.

Important Tips

  • Your first test might not be perfect: Don't expect perfection on the first try. The AI improves significantly with each refinement of your training data. Most users get it right after 2–3 test cycles.
  • Detailed training = better AI: Business owners who spend 15–20 minutes on their training form get dramatically better results than those who spend 2 minutes. The investment pays off immediately.
  • Run a week of supervised automation: For the first week, check conversations daily and refine your training data. After that, weekly reviews are sufficient for most businesses.
