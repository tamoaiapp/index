Step by Step: WhatsApp Auto-Reply Setup Guide

March 22, 2026  ·  6 min read

WhatsApp Business comes with a basic auto-reply feature, but it has significant limitations: it only sends a single generic message, it can't answer specific questions, and it doesn't learn about your business. Customers quickly recognize these templated replies as impersonal — and it often feels worse than no reply at all.

A real WhatsApp auto-reply setup uses AI to generate responses that actually answer what the customer asked. In this guide, you'll learn how to set up intelligent auto-replies that feel natural, provide accurate information, and work 24/7 — all for free.

Why You Need This

The difference between a template auto-reply ("Thanks for contacting us, we'll reply soon!") and an intelligent AI reply ("Hi! Our handmade earrings are priced between $15–$45. The blue collection you might have seen in our last post is currently in stock. Would you like to know more?") is enormous. One builds trust and moves customers toward a purchase. The other just confirms someone received their message.

TamoWork sets up this intelligent auto-reply system on your PC for free. Its AI employee understands what customers are asking and provides specific, helpful answers based on your business training data — no templates, no generic responses.

Step by Step: How to Do It

Step 1

Download TamoWork

Go to tamowork.com and download the free Windows installer. Run it on your PC. Installation takes about 3 minutes. The app opens with a dashboard showing your available AI employees.

Step 2

Select WhatsApp Employee and Connect

Click "WhatsApp Employee" from the dashboard. When the QR code appears, open WhatsApp on your phone, go to Linked Devices, and scan it. This links your WhatsApp account to TamoWork. The connection stays active as long as your phone has internet access.

Step 3

Create Your Business Knowledge Base

This is the core of your auto-reply setup. Write comprehensive business information in the training form: every product and its price, all your service options, your delivery area and costs, your payment methods, your operating hours, and specific answers to your most frequently asked questions. Use clear, natural language — the AI will use this to compose replies.

Step 4

Configure Greeting Messages

Set up how the AI employee greets new customers. A good greeting: uses the customer's name if available, acknowledges what they said, and immediately provides helpful information. Avoid greetings that just say "hello" without substance — customers want information, not pleasantries.

Step 5

Define Escalation Conditions

Configure TamoWork to escalate certain types of messages to you personally: complaints, requests for order status, payment issues, and anything else that requires your specific attention. All other messages get handled by the AI auto-reply system. This keeps you focused while maintaining quality.

Step 6

Test Multiple Conversation Types

Test your auto-reply setup with at least 5 different types of customer messages. Send a price inquiry. Ask about delivery. Request a product recommendation. Ask an unusual question. Send a complaint. Evaluate every response and refine your training data before going live.

Step 7

Go Live and Maintain

Activate the WhatsApp Employee. Your intelligent auto-reply system is now live. Check conversations daily for the first two weeks, then move to weekly reviews as the system stabilizes. Each review session is an opportunity to improve your training data and make the AI employee even more effective.

Important Tips

  • AI auto-replies are far superior to templates: Unlike basic auto-reply tools, TamoWork generates unique responses to each message based on its content. This feels natural to customers and produces far better outcomes.
  • The greeting sets the tone: The first message a new customer receives from you shapes their perception of your business. Make sure your greeting is warm, informative, and professional.
  • Refine based on real conversations: The best training data comes from reading actual customer conversations and identifying where the AI fell short. Make this a regular practice.
  • Completely free, no limits: TamoWork doesn't limit the number of auto-replies you can send. Handle 10 messages or 1,000 — the cost is always zero.
