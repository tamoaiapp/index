Frequently asked questions

❓ What is the best background for product photos?

Pure white (#FFFFFF) is the e-commerce standard. It eliminates distractions, makes products pop, and is required by Amazon and most major marketplaces for main product images. For secondary photos, light grey or lifestyle backgrounds work well.

❓ Do I need a professional camera for product photography?

No. Modern smartphones with 12MP+ cameras produce quality sufficient for any marketplace. What matters most is lighting (natural window light) and a clean background. TamoWork AI handles the rest automatically.

❓ How many photos should I have per product?

The recommended minimum is 4-5 photos: front, side, back, material/quality detail, and at least one lifestyle or in-use photo. Products with 5+ photos have 2-3x higher conversion rates than single-photo listings.
