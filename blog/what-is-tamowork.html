What Is TamoWork?

TamoWork is a local AI employee — a desktop application that runs directly on your Windows computer and automatically replies to your Instagram comments, Instagram Direct Messages, and WhatsApp messages on your behalf.

Think of it as hiring a digital employee who already knows your business, speaks your customers' language, and is always available — without any monthly subscription, API key, or technical expertise required.

Unlike SaaS tools that charge $50–$200/month and send your data to external servers, TamoWork runs entirely on your machine. Your conversations, your customer data, your business information — all of it stays on your computer.

How It Works

TamoWork uses a local AI model (Ollama + LLaMA) that runs entirely on your computer. There's no cloud, no monthly fee, no data sent to third-party servers. You install it once, configure your business information, and it starts working immediately.

Here's the setup process in three steps:

  • Download and install TamoWork on your Windows PC
  • Connect your accounts — Instagram and/or WhatsApp
  • Describe your business — products, prices, tone of voice

That's it. Your AI employee is now online and handling customer messages while you focus on what you do best: running your business.
