WhatsApp Auto Response Examples for Small Business

🗓 March 22, 2026⏱ 7 min read🏷 WhatsApp, Auto-Reply, Templates

Writing good auto-responses is harder than it looks. Too robotic and customers feel ignored. Too vague and you waste everyone's time. The goal is a message that feels like it came from a real person who genuinely knows your business — and that moves the conversation forward instead of stalling it.

Below are 10 real auto-response templates for the most common WhatsApp scenarios small businesses face every day. Each one includes the typical customer message that triggers it, the ideal auto-reply, and notes on why it works.

What Makes a Good Auto-Response?

Before the examples, a quick framework. The best auto-responses share four qualities:

  • They acknowledge the specific question — not just "thanks for your message"
  • They provide real information — price ranges, timeframes, options
  • They set expectations — when will a human follow up, if needed?
  • They invite the next step — a question, a link, a simple call to action

Now let's get into the examples.

1. Price Inquiry

Customer message
"Hi, how much does it cost?"
Auto-response
Hi! Thanks for reaching out. Our prices depend on the product and quantity — the range is generally $X to $Y. Could you tell me which item you're interested in? I'll send you the exact price right away. 😊

Why it works: It gives a real range instead of a non-answer, and asks a clarifying question that keeps the conversation moving.

2. Shipping and Delivery

Customer message
"Do you ship to [city]? How long does delivery take?"
Auto-response
Yes, we ship nationwide! Delivery usually takes 3 to 7 business days depending on your location. Shipping costs vary by region — once you're ready to order, just send me your zip code and I'll get you the exact estimate. 📦

3. Custom Order Request

Customer message
"Can I order a custom version of this? I want it in blue with my logo on it."
Auto-response
Absolutely, we do custom orders! For personalized pieces, we need a few details: the quantity you need, your preferred colors, and any artwork or logo files you want included. Custom orders typically take 7–10 business days to produce. Want to get started? Share the details and I'll prepare a quote for you.

4. Out of Stock

Customer message
"Is the red one still available?"
Auto-response
The red version is currently out of stock, but we're expecting a new batch within 10–15 days. Would you like me to put you on the waiting list? I'll message you as soon as it's back. In the meantime, we have it available in blue and black if you'd like to take a look.

Why it works: It doesn't just say "no." It offers a waitlist, a timeline, and alternatives — three ways to keep the sale alive.

5. Business Hours

Customer message
"Are you open right now? Can I call?"
Auto-response
Our team is available Monday to Saturday, 9 AM to 6 PM. If you're reading this outside those hours, don't worry — I'll personally follow up first thing when we're back. In the meantime, feel free to describe what you need and I'll have everything ready for when we talk. 🙌

6. Payment Methods

Customer message
"What payment methods do you accept?"
Auto-response
We accept credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard), bank transfer, PayPal, and cash on delivery for local orders. All card payments are processed securely. Is there a specific payment method you were wondering about? Happy to give you more details.

7. Return and Exchange Policy

Customer message
"What's your return policy if I don't like the product?"
Auto-response
We offer exchanges and returns within 7 days of delivery, as long as the product is in its original condition. If there's a defect or damage, we cover the full replacement at no cost to you. Just send us a photo of the issue and we'll sort it out quickly. Your satisfaction is the priority. ✅

8. Appointment Booking

Customer message
"I'd like to schedule a consultation / appointment."
Auto-response
Great, let's get that set up! We have openings on Monday, Wednesday, and Friday between 10 AM and 4 PM. Which day works best for you? Once you pick a time, I'll confirm your slot and send you the details. Looking forward to it! 📅

9. Wholesale or Bulk Order

Customer message
"Do you sell in bulk? I need 50 units for my store."
Auto-response
Yes, we work with wholesale clients! Orders of 20+ units qualify for our bulk pricing — typically 15–25% off depending on the quantity and product. To give you an accurate quote, I just need to know which products you're interested in and your preferred delivery timeline. Let's talk numbers! 📊

10. General Inquiry (No Specific Topic)

Customer message
"Hi, I found you on Instagram and wanted to learn more."
Auto-response
Hey! Thanks for reaching out — great to hear from you. We specialize in [your product/service] for [your target customer]. I'd love to help you find exactly what you need. What were you looking for? Once I know more, I can point you to the right options and pricing. 😊

Tips for Writing Your Own Auto-Responses

These examples are starting points, not scripts. The best auto-responses are tailored to your specific business, products, and customers. Here are a few principles to guide you:

  • Match your usual tone: If you naturally use emojis and casual language, use them. If you're more formal, keep it professional. Consistency builds trust.
  • Avoid generic phrases: "Thank you for contacting us" signals automation. Start with something more personal and specific to the question.
  • Always end with a question or next step: A response that ends with no clear next action kills momentum. Always give the customer something to do.
  • Update responses regularly: Your prices, stock, and policies change. Set a reminder to review your auto-responses monthly.
  • Test them yourself: Send the messages to your own number and read them as a customer. Do they feel helpful or hollow?

The goal isn't to replace human conversation — it's to make sure every customer gets an immediate, useful first response that keeps the door open until you can take over personally.
